About

Run by Anis Bara, painting NW London properly.

Started on the brushes young, learned the trade through bigger London outfits, and went out on his own when the demand stopped fitting in evenings and weekends.

The way Anis sees it, anyone can put paint on a wall. The job is in the prep — sanded, filled, dust pulled, edges crisp, and lines that hold up under a south-facing window in summer.

Domestic and commercial across NW1 through NW11, plus W and HA postcodes. Fully insured, free quotes, references available on request.

How it works

Four steps from first call to final coat.

Step · 01

Have a chat

Phone, WhatsApp or the form. Tell us the rooms, the timing, the kind of finish you're after.

Step · 02

Quote on site

Anis comes round, takes proper measurements, checks the surfaces, and writes you a fixed price. No vague estimates.

Step · 03

Start work

Floors and furniture covered, dust extraction where needed, prep done before a single drop of paint goes anywhere.

Step · 04

Walk through

We finish, snag, and walk every room with you. If anything's off, we put it right before we leave.
