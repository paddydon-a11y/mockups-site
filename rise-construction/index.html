What We Do

Construction Services

01

Bespoke Extensions

Single and two-phase extensions designed to transform your home. From initial groundworks through to handover, we manage every stage with precision and minimal disruption to your daily life.

02

Oak Frame Construction

Bespoke oak framed structures built on site by hand. Balconies, carports, garden rooms and more — each frame is crafted from carefully selected green oak and finished to an exceptional standard.

03

Loft Conversions

Full loft conversions that add valuable living space to your home. Dormer, hip-to-gable, and Velux conversions all completed to building regulations with structural integrity guaranteed.

04

New Builds & Developments

Ground-up residential construction and development projects. From planning through to completion, we deliver new builds to the highest specification with meticulous attention to every detail.

05

Roofing & Structural Work

Traditional cut roofs, structural steelwork, and complete re-roofing projects. We specialise in complex roof structures that require real skill and experience to execute properly.

06

Renovations & Refurbishments

Complete property renovations that breathe new life into tired homes. Period restorations, full refurbishments, and modernisation projects handled with care and expertise.

How We Work

From Vision to Reality

1

Consultation

We visit your property, discuss your vision, assess the site and provide honest guidance on scope, timescales and budget.

2

Design

Detailed plans, material selection, and structural design. We work with trusted architects and engineers to ensure everything is right before a single brick is laid.

3

Build

Construction carried out by our own skilled team — not subcontractors. The owner is on site every day, personally overseeing quality at every stage.

4

Handover

Full snagging, final inspection, and a proper handover with all certificates and guarantees. We only sign off when you are completely satisfied.
