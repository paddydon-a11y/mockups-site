The Trades We Cover

Seven crafts, one team.

Most builders juggle subcontractors. We handle the lot in-house, so the guy doing the tiling answers to the guy doing the plumbing, and the kitchen fitter already knows what the tiler needs.

I.

Bathrooms

Full bathroom installs from rip-out to tiled finish. Wet rooms, en-suites, downstairs cloakrooms. Plumbing, tiling, electrics all done by us.

II.

Kitchen fitting

Supplied or customer-bought. Units, worktops, splashbacks, appliances. Tiled and sealed to showroom standard.

III.

Tiling

Large-format porcelain, marble, mosaic. Straight lay or pattern. Floors, walls, splashbacks. Grouted clean, not smudged.

IV.

Flooring

Laminate, engineered wood, LVT, tile. Including stair treads and winders done properly, not bodged.

V.

Plumbing

Bathroom plumbing, kitchen plumbing, leak repair, radiator moves. Gas work brought in through a Gas Safe partner when needed.

VI.

Carpentry

Doors, skirting, architrave, stud walls, bespoke media walls, wardrobes. The finish work that makes a room feel finished.

VII.

Building & renovation

Full refurbs, extensions, loft conversions, structural openings. One team from strip-out to snagging.
