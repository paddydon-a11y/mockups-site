8 things, done well ✿
01

Front elevations

Render, masonry, brick, sills, downpipes. Two coats, neat lines, no overspray on the slabs.

02

Garden rooms & cabins

Timber rooms repainted in heritage colours. Breathable coatings that survive London winters.

03

Sheds (yes, sheds)

Tired old shed? Knackered fence panels? Mossed-up summerhouse? Bring it on.

04

Sash windows

Eased, burnt off, repainted in proper heritage colours. By hand, the way they ought to be.

05

Fences & decking

Cuprinol, Osmo or Ronseal. Brushed not sprayed where the fiddly bits matter.

06

Interior repaints

Yes, we do indoor too. Full house, single rooms, ceilings, woodwork. Dust sheets always.

07

Kitchen cabinets

New look, no new kitchen. Sanded, primed, sealed for a proper hard-wearing finish.

08

Free colour visit

Can’t pick a shade? We’ll bring samples, paint them on the wall, no pressure.
