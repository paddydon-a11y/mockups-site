What we do

Two sides of the
same property.

We rebuild what's inside the walls, then carry the same standard out to the patio, the pergola and the driveway. Most clients book us for both because the join between house and garden is where most projects fall down.

01 / Renovations

Inside the house

Full-house refurbishments, kitchen rebuilds, rear extensions, vaulted ceilings, plastering, second-fix joinery and finish. We lift floors, knock through, replumb and rewire where needed. Project managed by Damian, never sub-let to a stranger.

02 / Landscaping

Outside the house

Indian sandstone and porcelain patios, decorative block paving, retaining sleepers, fencing, decking, pergolas with integrated lighting, and resin-bound driveways. We dig out properly, lay sub-base on grade and finish dead-flat.

How we work

No surprises,
no chasing.

Every job runs through the same four steps. You always know who's on your job, what's happening this week, and where the budget sits.

01

Site visit

Damian visits, takes measurements and photos, listens. Free, no obligation.

02

Written quote

Itemised quote within a week. We break out materials, labour and contingency so you can compare like for like.

03

On site

Stage payments, weekly walkthroughs, photos of anything covered up. We protect floors, manage waste and lock up at night.

04

Handover

Snag list together, signed off together. One year on workmanship, manufacturer warranty on materials.
