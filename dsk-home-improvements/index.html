The Team · 03

A local outfit run by Davinder Singh from Hounslow. Honest quotes, tidy fits, finished on the day we said we would.

Most of what we do is windows and doors fitted around West London — uPVC, aluminium and composite. The handyman side covers the in-between jobs: a hinge that's dropped, a door that won't shut, a fence panel down. If you want one number for the lot, that's us.

Quotes are free, written, and there's no pressure on the call. If we can't get to it in the week, we'll say so on the phone.
