How we work

A clear process, start to finish.

Every project we run, no matter how small, follows the same four stages. You always know where you are in the build.

01

Initial chat

Site visit or call. We talk about what you want to achieve, your budget bracket, and whether it's likely to need planning.

02

Design & pricing

Drawings, planning where needed, cost plan and a full written quote. No grey areas, no hidden extras.

03

Build

One project manager, our long-standing trades and weekly progress updates. We work clean and keep neighbours sweet.

04

Hand over

Full snag walk, all certificates, NHBC sign-off on new builds, and we're at the end of the phone long after you've moved in.

About Lime

A small office in Longton. Big builds across the North West.

Lime Property Group has been building homes across Lancashire for over a decade. We’re based at our office on Chapel Lane in Longton. Small enough that the same people answer the phone, run the site and sign off the work.

Our setup is deliberate. A select group of architects, structural engineers, planners and subcontractors we’ve worked with for years. Same faces, every project. That’s why our finish is consistent and our programmes hold.
