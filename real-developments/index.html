What we do

Six things we do properly, every job.

01 / Foundations

Concrete foundations & slabs

Strip and trench foundations, raft slabs, oversite concrete and floor slabs. Set out to engineer's drawings, poured and finished to building control sign-off.

02 / Groundworks

Site clearance & muck-away

Strip, dig and grade. Soak-aways, reduced levels, subbase compaction. Hardcore in, spoil out, ready for the next trade in.

03 / Brickwork

Brick & block

Face brickwork, blockwork, retaining walls and feature courses. Time-served bricklayers, neat joints, clean finishes.

04 / Drainage

Foul & surface drainage

New drain runs, soak-aways, ACO channels, manhole connections. Pressure-tested before backfill, signed off by Building Control.

05 / Fencing

Fencing & boundaries

Closeboard, post-and-rail, panel and steel. Concrete posts, set to line, plumb and level. No dipping panels.

06 / Hard Landscaping

Patios & driveways

Porcelain patios, block paving driveways, resin-bound and natural stone. Properly bedded, jointed and sealed.
