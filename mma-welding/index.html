What We Do

Proper welding, on site or in the shop.

Manual metal arc and TIG welding for marine, structural and pipe work jobs. Coded to the standard the job demands, from a hull plate to a hand-rail.

MMA Stick Welding

Manual metal arc welding to spec, on heavy steel, dirty steel and outdoor work where TIG can't keep up. Hand-blended root and cap.

TIG Welding

Clean fusion TIG on stainless, mild steel and aluminium. For food-grade pipe, hygienic process and marine fit-out work.

Marine Welding

Hull plates, bilge frames, over-plating, narrowboat refits and rebuilds. Built my own boat from scratch, now living on it in Gravesend.

Structural Welding

Steel frames, RSJ work, columns and beams. Welded out on London builds and across the M25 corridor.

Pipe Welding

Spool fabrication, root and cap on carbon and stainless pipe. Process, riser, drainage and storm work for site teams.

Custom Fabrication

Brackets, frames, hand-rails, plinths and one-off builds. Bring a drawing or a back-of-an-envelope sketch.

On-Site Mobile Welding

Mobile rig, generator, full PPE. Welding where the work is, from sites across London out to the Kent and Essex coast.

Narrowboat Refit & Repair

Cabin tops, plate repairs, fuel tanks. Twenty years on the canals, a boat builder as well as a welder.

About Mike

Run by Mike Foulkes. A welder who built his own boat.

Mike's Mobile Arc Welding is a one-man outfit based in Gravesend, Kent. I cover the M25 and out across the South East, taking on marine, structural and pipe work jobs from coded site work to one-off fabrication.

The boat in the photo is mine. I built her from scratch over a few years, plate by plate, weld by weld. She floats, she runs, and she's where I live now, moored up in Gravesend. There's not much I haven't welded by this point.

  • MMA, TIG and MIG kit in the van, ready for on-site jobs.
  • Marine, structural and pipe work welcomed, big jobs or small.
  • Straight quotes, no VAT shock at the end, no chasing for an invoice.
