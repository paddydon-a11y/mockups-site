01 / What we build

Every kind of wall, laid to the line

From a new-build gable to a garden wall, a repoint or a brick-paved path, every job is laid by hand and built to last. No subbing out the bits that matter, no shortcuts on the parts you cannot see.

New build & extensions

Footings to roofline. Cavity walls, gable ends, piers and chimneys laid plumb, level and to the drawings.

Garden & boundary walls

Retaining walls, raised beds, piers and copings. Built to hold ground and look sharp from the street.

Repointing & restoration

Lime and sand-cement repointing, brick matching and rebuilds. Bring tired London brickwork back to life.

Patios, paths & steps

Herringbone and basket-weave brick paving, brick steps and edging. Hard landscaping that beds in for good.

Brickwork repairs & rebuilds

Cracked walls, blown brickwork, bowed bays and bulging parapets put right and rebuilt to match.

Feature & bespoke work

Arches, curved walls, brick BBQs and planters. The interesting jobs other bricklayers turn down.

02 / Down to the last brick

Old London brick, matched so the repair disappears

Half the skill in this city is making new work look like it has always been there. We source reclaimed and matched stock brick, set the bond to the original and point it to suit, so a repair or extension reads as one with the house, not a patch bolted on.

Brick, bond and mortar matched to the original

04 / The people behind it

Meet ZillaWorks

We are a small, time-served bricklaying outfit based in London. Between us we have spent years on every face of the trade, from new-build sites and extensions to fiddly garden features and period repointing.

When you ring ZillaWorks you deal with the people who turn up, price the work and lay every brick. We keep the site tidy, keep you in the loop, and we do not leave until it is right.

Most of our work comes from people passing our name on, which tells you what you need to know. If you want brickwork that still looks good in twenty years, give us a shout.
