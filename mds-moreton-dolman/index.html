About Jeremy

Electrician, carpenter, problem-solver.

M.D.S is run by me, Jeremy. I'm an electrician and a carpenter, which means I can handle the jobs most tradesmen have to split between two.

What I really care about is older properties. The Victorians and Edwardians built with timber, lime and lath, and modern renovation techniques don't always get on with them. I've spent years learning what goes wrong in 100-year-old houses, and how to put it right without making the next problem worse.

I'm currently finishing the electrical fit-out on two extensions and have just completed a full renovation of a 2-bed mid-terrace. I also do quick-turnaround kitchen and bathroom refits for landlords who need the place let before the next month starts.

If you've got an older house that's been giving you grief, or a rental that needs a straightforward refresh, get in touch. I'll tell you honestly whether I can help.
