About MK Brickwork

Craftsmanship You Can Trust

MK Brickwork is a professional bricklaying company serving Guildford and the surrounding Surrey areas. Run by Mick Keane, we take pride in delivering quality workmanship on every project we take on.

From new build homes and extensions to garden walls, brick steps and repair work — no job is too big or too small. We specialise in decorative banding and feature brickwork that adds real character to any property.

Every project is completed to the highest standard, on time and with minimal disruption. We believe in honest pricing, clear communication and brickwork you’ll be proud of for years to come.
