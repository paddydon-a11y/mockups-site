What we do

Any aspect of roofing, sorted

Gutters, fascias & soffits

Cleared, cleaned or fully replaced, along with cladding, so the front of the house looks right again.

Ridges & valleys

Loose or cracked ridge tiles and tired valleys repointed or replaced to keep the water out.

Tiles, felt & battens

From a single slipped tile to a full re-roof, refelted and rebattened the right way.

Flat roofs

Rubber (EPDM), fibreglass (GRP) or felt flat roofs laid to last on extensions, garages and porches.

Gutter clearing & jet wash

Gutters emptied and flushed, plus a jet wash for roofs, frames and dirty render.

Repairs & emergencies

Leaks, storm damage and odd jobs at short notice, often the same week you call.
