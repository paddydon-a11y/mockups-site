What we do

One outfit for the full interior package.

Suspended Ceilings

Concealed and exposed grid systems. Commercial-spec installs across hospitals, offices and retail.

Drylining & Partitions

Metal stud, taping & jointing, fire-rated and acoustic partitions ready for paint.

MF Ceilings

Metal furring framing for flat plasterboard ceilings. Clean lines, level finish, ready for skim.

Plastering

External Rendering
