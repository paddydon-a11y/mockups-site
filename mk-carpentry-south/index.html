What We Do

Fire safety work, done to the standard it's meant to be.

Fire doors are a joiner's job taken to a life-safety level, fitted to the door's certification, with the right frame, seals and ironmongery. That's the heart of what we do, alongside the passive protection and inspections that keep a building compliant.

Fire Doors

Supply, installation and replacement of certified FD30 and FD60 fire doorsets for communal areas, flats, offices and commercial premises. Frame, intumescent and smoke seals, hinges, closers and gaps all set to the door's certification.

Fire Protection

Fire Control Services

About Us

Croydon-based fire protection, run hands-on.

M&K Carpentry (South) Limited is a Croydon-based business specialising in fire doors and fire protection across the South East. What began in carpentry and joinery now centres on the work that matters most in a building: keeping fire and smoke where it should be.

Fire doors and passive protection only do their job when they're fitted properly, to the right standard, and kept that way. That's the line we hold on every job: careful installation, honest advice and clear records, for homeowners, landlords, managing agents and commercial clients alike.
