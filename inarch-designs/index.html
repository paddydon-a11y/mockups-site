How we work

Four stages.
No surprises.

From first call to last snag, the same studio walks the project through. You'll deal with the same project lead from feasibility to handover.

Step 01

Brief & feasibility

A site visit, a conversation about what you actually want, and an honest read on what's achievable. Free of charge, no obligation either way.

Step 02

Design & planning

Concept drawings, refinement with you, then planning submission. We handle building regs and any pre-app meetings with the council.

Step 03

Build & manage

Programmed, costed, managed by the same studio. One project lead, one phone number, the same team you met on day one.

Step 04

Handover & aftercare

Snag-free handover, a written aftercare commitment and a building you can stop thinking about. We come back to fix what needs fixing.
