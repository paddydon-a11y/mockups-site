// 01 Field Notes

A Manchester outfit that builds for the long haul.

Legacy Decking started on a back-garden in Bolton. Ten years and a couple of hundred builds later, the rule has not moved: build it the way you'd build it for your own house.

Composite grey, fresh timber, glass balustrade overlooking the Pennines. We do the lot. Design, build, sleeper walls, paving, the ugly groundwork before any of the pretty stuff. Run by Daniel, every job is supervised on-site. Quote in person, no pressure, fixed price.

// 03 The Build

Eight things we do properly,
nothing we don't.

LD/01
Composite Decking

Trex, Millboard, Cladco, Saige. Stone grey, anthracite, walnut. Anti-slip, splinter-free, 25-year guaranteed.

LD/02
Timber Decking

Pressure-treated softwood and hardwood. Built on heavy joists, ground-screw or concrete pad foundations.

LD/03
Glass Balustrades

10mm toughened glass, satin-stainless or post-mounted. The clean view that turns a deck into a viewing terrace.

LD/04
Sleeper Walls

Pressure-treated oak or pine sleepers. Retaining walls, raised beds, stepped terraces on awkward sloping plots.

LD/05
Indian Stone Paving

Sandstone, limestone, granite. Cut, laid and pointed. Patios, paths, full back-garden landscaping.

LD/06
Garden Rooms

Insulated timber-frame studios on full base groundworks. Fitted out, wired, and finished with the deck.

LD/07
Pergolas + Awnings

Bespoke louvre roofs, slatted shade structures, sail awnings. Aluminium or timber, built to size.

LD/08
Fencing + Gates

Closeboard, slatted contemporary, hit-and-miss. Posts in concrete, gates hung true. No compromises.

LD/+
Got something stranger? Ask us.

Hot tub bases, koi pond surrounds, rooftop terraces. If it's outside and made of timber or stone, we've probably built one.
