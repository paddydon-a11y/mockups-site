What we do.

All aspects of brickwork
01
New builds
Working with developers and main contractors. Substructure to roofline. Consistent gauge, clean perps, programme on time.
02
Extensions
Single and double storey. Full brickwork from footings up, openings cut clean, lintels in level. Tied into the existing course every time.
03
Garden walls
Straight, curved, piered, decorative. Built on proper concrete pads, capped properly, designed to outlast the fence next door.
04
Re-pointing
Old mortar raked out properly, fresh lime or sand-cement repointed to match the original. Restoring period houses and Victorian boundary walls.
05
Slabbing & paving
Indian sandstone, porcelain and block paving. Laid on a base that drains, jointed properly, edge-restrained so it stays put.
06
Porches & piers
Brick-built porches, gate piers, pillars and feature elements matched to your existing brickwork. Tidy joints, considered detailing.
07
Boundary & retaining
Tall boundary runs and retaining walls. Reinforced where loaded, drained where needed, designed to hold a slope for decades.
08
Repairs & rebuilds
Storm damage, settlement cracks, blown bricks, leaning chimneys. Sympathetic rebuilds that match the original so you cannot see the join.

About Mason

Owner on every job.

Build Right is run by Mason Barnes. Coventry born, Coventry based, on the tools every day. There's no office, no middleman, no answering service. You ring, Mason picks up.

That matters because brickwork is detail work. Gauge, perps, plumb, level, the right mortar mix for the brick and the weather. Get the boring stuff right and the wall stands for a hundred years. Get it wrong and everyone can see.

If you've got a job in Coventry, the West Midlands or Warwickshire and you want it built right the first time, send the photos and we'll come and look.
