What we do

Three specialisms,
one set of hands.

From a single feature wall to a full re-render, the workflow is the same: prep properly, finish patiently, and leave the place tidier than we found it. No subcontractors, no rushed seconds — just the hand that started the job seeing it through to the last brushstroke.

01

Wallpaper & Venetian plaster

Decorative finishes
Polished Venetian plaster, micro-cement, hand-applied wallpaper and statement feature walls. Every layer trowelled, burnished and sealed in a single set of hands — the result is the kind of surface you want to keep touching.
  • Marmorino
  • Stucco
  • Tadelakt
  • Hand-hung wallpaper
  • Gold leaf
02

Interior painting

Lime wash, texture, wood
Full-room repaints, feature walls, woodwork and trim. We carry lime wash for chalky, breathable heritage walls; texture paint for tactile depth; and a careful hand on stained or painted wood. Quiet prep work is half the job.
  • Lime wash
  • Texture paint
  • Wood paint & stain
  • Heritage colour matching
03

Exterior painting
& rendering

Silicone, acrylic, lime
Masonry paint, weatherboards, windows and doors — plus full silicone, acrylic and lime render systems. We pick the system to match the substrate (and the listing if there is one), then leave a finish that endures the British weather.
  • Silicone render
  • Acrylic render
  • Lime render
  • Masonry paint
  • Window finishing

The Craft

Loyalty, punctuality
& the long view.

Centaurus Construction is built on years on the tools across heritage and contemporary projects. The detail comes first — we tailor what we use and how we use it to each room, each substrate, each client.

We turn up when we said we would, work tidily, and finish what we started. Most of the work comes from people who’ve had us before, or someone they trust. That is the standard we hold ourselves to.
