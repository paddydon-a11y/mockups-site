About Holdstock

Run by Levi, on the tools every job

Holdstock Carpentry was set up in 2021 by Levi Holdstock, a Colchester joiner who cares as much about the joints you don't see as the finish you do.

Every job is measured, made and fitted with the same care, whether it's a single fitted wardrobe or a full kitchen. You deal with Levi from the first measure to the final clean-up, and the work speaks for itself.
