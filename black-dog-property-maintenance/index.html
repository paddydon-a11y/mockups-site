What we do

One trusted team for the whole house

Full refits are our bread and butter, but property maintenance means exactly that: if it needs fixing, fitting or freshening up, give us a shout.

Bathroom ripout & refit

The full journey: strip out to bare walls, first fix plumbing, tanking boards, trays, tiling and sparkling second fix. We handle the mess so you just enjoy the finish.

Kitchen refits

New units, worktops, splashbacks, appliances and lighting, fitted around your plans and your budget.

Tiling & flooring

Wall and floor tiling, marble-effect porcelain, metro splashbacks and new floors, including underfloor heating.

Painting & decorating

Fresh, tidy finishes inside and out. Preparation done properly so the paint lasts.

Plumbing & repairs

First and second fix plumbing on our refits, plus everyday fixes: leaks, taps, toilets and radiators.

Odd jobs & maintenance

Shelves, doors, sealant, silicone, garden jobs, the snagging list. The little things that never get done: we do them.
