About SJLK

Local builders. High-end results.

Hands-on builders covering Kent, London and Essex. Luxury renovations, full groundworks, bricklaying, paving and the careful detail that separates a finished home from a fixed-up one.

SJLK Construction Group is a small, family-run outfit. We take on a manageable number of jobs at any one time so the directors are still on site, and the work doesn't get handed off to whoever's free that week. Most of what comes through is repeat or referral.

The work splits roughly three ways. Foundations and groundworks for new builds and extensions. Heavy bricklaying, boundary walls and structural blockwork. And the visible finish — patios, garden landscaping, curved steps, repointing, the bits clients actually look at every day.

If you've got a build coming up across the South East and you'd rather use a small firm that turns up when it says it will, get in touch.

What we take on

Six trades, handled in-house.

From the dig and the pour to the last brick laid. We do less subcontracting than most outfits this size, which means tighter timelines and a finish we can stand behind.

01 · Builds

Luxury builds

High-end residential builds and extensions. Project managed end-to-end. We take on a small number at a time so the directors are still on site, every day.

02 · Groundworks

Groundworks & foundations

Excavation, foundations, drainage, slab pours and footings. The bit you only see once but live with for the next fifty years. Worth getting right first time.

03 · Bricklaying

Bricklaying & blockwork

Boundary walls, pillars, garden walls, structural blockwork and matching extensions. Curved work and decorative detail are a speciality.

04 · Patios

Patios & paving

Block paving, porcelain paving, slate, sandstone and reclaimed brick patios. Laid on proper sub-base, pointed cleanly, finished to last.

05 · Landscaping

Landscaping

Steps, retaining walls, fencing, garden remodels, planting beds and hard landscaping. We design and build the lot, end to end.

06 · Renovations

Renovations

Whole-house refurbishments, period property reworks and structural alterations. Honest, sequenced and managed by people who know what they're doing.

How we work

Four stages. No surprises.

Most builders disappear once the deposit lands. We sequence every job into four clear stages so you always know where you are and what's next.

1

Site visit

We come and see it. Free, honest, no pitch. We'll tell you what's possible and what isn't before any quote is written.

2

Fixed quote

Itemised written quote with materials, labour and a realistic programme. The number on the page is the number on the invoice.

3

Build phase

Daily site presence from a director. Weekly photo updates and a clean site at the end of every day, every week.

4

Sign off

Snag walk, photos, certificates and the keys back. A 12-month workmanship guarantee follows you afterwards.
