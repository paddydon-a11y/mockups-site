Hi, I'm Ben.

25 years in Burnley lad Commercial Heritage Domestic Out-of-hours

I started on the glass at seventeen — and I've been on it ever since. Ben Slater Glazing began life as a van and a phone and a habit of turning up. Twenty-five years later it's grown, a little, but the phone's still the same, and it still rings straight through to me.

We fit shop fronts and fire-rated screens for commercial clients, we re-lead stained glass panels for Grade II listed churches, and on a Tuesday tea-time we'll board up a broken pane on a family front door. All of it matters. None of it gets sent off to somebody we don't know.

If you've got a building with a bit of glass that needs sorting — old, new, smashed, scheduled, specced or scribbled on the back of a receipt — give us a shout. We'll come and have a look.
