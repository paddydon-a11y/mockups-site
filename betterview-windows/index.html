How it works

One visit, one quote, one fit date.

How a typical Betterview job runs from the first call to the cup of tea after we have swept up.

01

Free home visit

You ring or message. We come round, measure up, talk colours and finishes, and answer any questions about specs, glass or trims.

02

Honest written quote

You get a clear, itemised price within a couple of days. No hard sell, no manager phoning back to drop the price. The quote you get is the price you pay.

03

Tidy fit, on the day

We give you a real fit date, not a window. The team turn up, fit, finish, hoover up and leave. You sign off when you are happy. Guarantee in the post.
