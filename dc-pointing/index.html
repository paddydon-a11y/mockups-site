What we do

Brick & stone, repointed properly.

Brick Repointing

Raked out, brushed clean, repointed with a matched mortar mix that locks the wall back up.

Stone Repointing

Sandstone, gritstone, millstone, random rubble. Lime or cement, whichever the wall needs.

Chimney Repoints

Full scaffold or roof-ladder access, stack repointed, flashings checked, pots and cowls set right.

Gable Ends & Full Walls

One elevation or the whole house, scaffold up, raked back to sound mortar, repointed straight through.

Lime Mortar Work

Period and listed property repointing in NHL or hot-mixed lime to keep the wall breathing.

Mortar Matching

Colour and sand grade dialled in so the new joints blend, not stand out, when the wall dries.

Before

Mortar bleeding out, joints washed back, hairline cracks running through every course. Damp creeping in behind.

After

Raked back 15 to 20mm, brushed, dampened, repointed in a colour-matched mix, tooled off, brushed clean.
