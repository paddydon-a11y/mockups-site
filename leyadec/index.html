About LeyaDec

The detail makes
the difference.

"We don't just paint walls — we transform spaces. Every project gets the same dedication, whether it's a single room or an entire property."

LeyaDec is a premium painting and decorating company based in Birmingham, serving clients across the West Midlands and beyond. We specialise in high-end residential interiors, bespoke wall panelling, designer wallpapering and commercial fit-outs.

Our portfolio speaks for itself — from luxury kitchens with hand-sprayed cabinetry to grand hallways with intricate panelling. We combine traditional craftsmanship with a sharp eye for contemporary design.
