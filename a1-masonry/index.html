The mason

Jon Paul McGurk

A1 Masonry is run by Jon Paul, on the tools since 1999 and trading on his own for the last five years. Every job is his own hands, from setting out to the last joint pointed.

The work is mostly sandstone, the heritage and conservation kind that wants matching, dressing and a feel for how old walls were built. New stone too, when the build calls for it.
