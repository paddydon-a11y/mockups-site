About Everstone

Cut. Polished. Permanent.

Everstone is a small, specialist outfit of stone masons fabricating granite, quartz and marble worktops for kitchens across the North West. We template, cut and install ourselves so the slab you see in the workshop is the slab that ends up in your kitchen.

We work direct with homeowners, kitchen fitters and developers. No call centres, no salesmen, no rushed timelines. Most jobs go from final template to finished install in two weeks.

What We Cut

Materials matter.

01 / Granite

Granite

Hard-wearing natural stone. Heat, scratch and stain resistant. Each slab is unique, no two kitchens look the same.

02 / Quartz

Quartz

Engineered stone in hundreds of finishes. Non-porous, easy maintenance, consistent pattern. Calacatta, Caesarstone, Silestone.

03 / Marble

Marble

Classic luxury for the right kitchen. Soft natural veining, warm tone, statement pieces. Sealed properly on install.

Dekton & sintered

Ultra-thin, ultra-strong porcelain composites. UV stable, suitable for outdoor kitchens and feature walls.
