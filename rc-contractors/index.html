About RC Contractors

Run by a contractor,

Reg Cuthbert has been running commercial and high-end residential projects for over twenty years. RC Contractors is a St Albans-based main contractor that handles the full programme, from tender and procurement through to final handover and sign-off.

Reg is directly involved in every project. Not a figurehead, not a salesman. If you call, you get Reg. If you need a site walk, Reg will be there. That is the only way to keep quality and programme under proper control on large-value projects.
