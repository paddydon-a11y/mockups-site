About us

Built on the tools, run by Bill

BT Carpentry Construction Group is led by Bill Taylor, a carpenter by trade based in West Malling, Kent. What started as bespoke carpentry has grown into an established construction group taking on loft conversions, extensions and commercial packages, while keeping the hands on standards of a working chippy.

Every project is priced honestly, planned properly and finished to the standard we would want in our own homes. You deal with Bill directly from first visit to final walk round.

  • Carpenter led, quality first
  • Domestic and commercial work
  • Own electricians and plumbers
