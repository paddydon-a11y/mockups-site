Why us

The stuff you hire a plasterer for.

Every finish is only as good as the prep under it, and the clean-up after it. Here's what you can bank on when you book Prestige Plastering N.W.

i.

Clean sheets, every room.

Floors, skirtings and furniture masked before a trowel comes out. We leave the room cleaner than we found it.

ii.

Prep you don't normally see.

Background scoring, PVA and bonding-coat on stubborn substrates. The reason the finish actually lasts.

iii.

Honest fixed-price quotes.

No surprises mid-job. You'll know exactly what's included, what isn't, and when we can fit you in.

iv.

Ready for the next trade.

Painters, sparks, plumbers — we leave walls dead-flat, edges crisp and coving clean so the next crew can crack on.
