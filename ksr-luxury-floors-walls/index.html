The craft

A luxury surface is built, not bought. Layers of polished plaster and poured resin, worked by hand until the finish catches and bends the light like stone and metal.

KSR specialises in the finishes most trades won't touch, mirror-deep Venetian polished plaster, seamless epoxy resin floors and worktops, and crisp through-coloured K Render. Alongside the showpieces, we still get the fundamentals right: flawless plastering and skimming that gives every finish a perfect base. Every job is mixed, applied and burnished by hand, so no two surfaces are ever the same.

How it's done

From bare surface to finished

01

Consult & sample

We talk through the look, the room and the light, then prepare samples in your chosen colours, sheens and finishes.

02

Prepare

Surfaces are cleaned, repaired and base-coated so the plaster, resin or render has a flawless, stable foundation.

03

Apply

Coats are applied by hand and trowel, each one shaping the depth, movement and colour of the finished surface.

04

Burnish & seal

The surface is polished to its final sheen and sealed, protected, wipeable and built to last for years.
