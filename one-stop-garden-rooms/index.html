About

A small team, every step of the build.

One Stop Garden Rooms is a small team of professional installers covering the whole garden-room build under one roof. Bases, frames, lining, electrics, decking, treatment and waste removal, all in one visit.

The point of the name is the brief: one phone call, one team, one finished room. No subcontract chain, no half-finished plot to live with for weeks.
