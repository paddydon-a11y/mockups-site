How it goes

Four steps.
That's it.

1

Tell us a size

Drop a WhatsApp with the size you want, where it's going, and what you fancy planting. Photo of the spot helps.

2

Quote & deposit

We'll send a price for the build and timber. Small deposit holds the slot, rest paid on collection.

3

Built in the shed

Cut, screwed, sanded and treated. Two weeks for standard sizes, up to three for the big ones.

4

Collect or drop

Pick it up from the workshop, or local drop within ten miles for a small fuel charge. Then plant the thing.

About Planter Home

A man,
a saw,
a shed.

Planter Home started as one project in the garden, then a mate asked for one, then his neighbour. Now it's a small side hustle out of a workshop just outside Chepstow.

Every planter is built by hand, screwed not nailed, sanded smooth, and treated to take Welsh weather. Solid timber, no MDF, no flat-pack.
