№ 02/ About

One builder, start to finish.

D. Evans-Hughes Construction is a building team based in Cae Clyd, Tregarth, just outside Bangor. We do kitchens, bathrooms, extensions and full house renovations. Joinery, plumbing, plastering, tiling and finish carpentry, all run by the same builder from start to finish.

Two sides to the work. Private jobs for homeowners across Gwynedd, anything from a single room re-fit to an attic conversion or a stone outbuilding brought back into use. And our own property developments, taking older houses back to the bones and bringing them out the other side at a proper standard.

One number to call. One person on the tools every day. Honest start dates, honest finish dates, tidy site every evening.
