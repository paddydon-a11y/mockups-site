01 / What we do

The full dry lining package, one team.

From first stud to final skim, the whole lining package sits with us. One team on site, so the programme isn't waiting on three different subbies and the finish lands ready for paint.
01
Metal Stud Partitions
Single, twin and staggered-stud partitions set out to drawing. Single and multi-board build-ups with deflection heads and openings framed ready for second fix.
02
Wall & Ceiling Lining
Dot & dab, direct bond and independent wall lining. Plasterboard, moisture board and insulated laminates fixed plumb and ready for finish.
03
MF & Suspended Ceilings
MF, bulkhead and suspended ceilings set to laser line. Service zones boxed in and coordinated with M&E so the grid never fights the first fix above it.
04
Tape, Joint & Skim Finish
Level 3 to 5 finishes ready for paint or wallcovering. Three-coat jointing, taped angles and full skim coats, sanded and signed off for the trades behind us.
05
Fire & Acoustic Systems
Fire-rated and acoustic partitions, shaft walls and ceiling build-ups installed to system spec, with the right boards, seals and detailing for sign-off.
06
Insulation & Boarding
Acoustic and thermal insulation packed to partitions and ceilings, plus straight, square boarding ready for tape and joint. Tidy work, swept up behind us.

03 / How we work

Priced fair, run tidy, finished on time.

Two hands you actually know on site. The same team prices the job, boards it and finishes it, so nothing gets lost between people.
Step 01

Price & programme

Send the drawings or get us on site. We come back quickly with a fair price and a clear idea of how the package sits against your programme.

Step 02

On the tools

Set out, framed, boarded and insulated to spec. We keep our area tidy, work to your sequence and flag anything that won't sit right before it's buried.

Step 03

Tape, skim & hand over

Joints taped, angles beaded, skim laid and sanded to the level you need. Walked off and handed over clean, ready for the trades coming behind us.
