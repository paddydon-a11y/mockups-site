A bit about us…

Painters who turn up, tidy up, and finish the job.

What we do

Pick a colour. We will do the rest.

Six things we do most weeks. If you cannot see what you are after, just ask.

01 / Most popular

Interior painting & decorating

Living rooms, hallways, kitchens, bedrooms. Fill, sand, prime, two coats. Edges that look hand-cut because they are.

02 / Curb appeal

Exterior painting

Front doors, render, brickwork, sash windows, soffits and fascias. Properly weather-prepped so it stays sharp.

03 / Statement walls

Wallpaper & removal

Hung straight, matched perfectly. Removing a previous owner's questionable choice? We do that too. Steamers and patience.

04 / Smooth finish

Spray painting

Doors, kitchen units, radiators, panelling. Glass-flat finishes you cannot get with a brush. Properly masked, properly extracted.

05 / Prep work

Plastering & wall repair

Cracks, dents, picture-hook holes, water-damaged corners. We sort the wall before we paint it, not the other way round.

06 / Detail work

Wood, stairs & trim

Skirting, architraves, bannisters, doors. Stripped, primed and finished in eggshell or satin so they last another decade.
