- **Brand navy:** `#003060` / deep `#04284f` (sampled off logo shield).
- **Accent orange:** `#f07000` / hover `#d9640a` (sampled off logo) — used sparingly for CTAs & highlights.
- **Display type:** Bricolage Grotesque (characterful grotesk — distinct from banned/saturated picks).
