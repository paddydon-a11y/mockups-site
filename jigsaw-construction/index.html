About Jigsaw

A quarter of a century of experience, in the corner of every self-build we touch.

Jigsaw Construction Services Ltd is a family-owned project management and construction company based in Budleigh Salterton, Devon. Over twenty-five years of hands-on experience mean we understand every stage of a self-build from the ground up.

We work with self-builders, private clients and contract partners to deliver new-build houses, extensions and renovations, built to the highest quality, within the time-frame and budget you set.

Services

Whether you're building a home of your own or a contract job needs steering through to handover, we wear both hats. Project manager and builder, on your side.

01 / 06

Self-Build Project Management

Building your own home is a dream for many. Our job is to make sure it doesn't become a nightmare. Planning, procurement, programme, trades, cost control, sign-off. We carry the stress so you can enjoy the build.

  • Planning & Building Regs liaison
  • Trade procurement & scheduling
  • Cost reporting & value engineering
  • Warranty & sign-off co-ordination
02 / 06

Contract Building

Full contract builds where we carry the risk, the labour and the delivery. One contract, one price, one programme. We lead the trades and hand you back the keys on a Friday.

  • Fixed-price contracts
  • Full trade management
  • On-site health & safety
  • Client reporting throughout
03 / 06

New Build Houses

Traditional masonry, oak frame, SIPS or timber frame. From ground-breaking to landscaped garden, we build houses designed to last a century, finished to a standard the next owner will still be grateful for.

  • Oak frame & traditional masonry
  • Groundworks & drainage
  • First & second fix trades
  • Full external works & landscaping
04 / 06

Extensions & Renovations

Adding to an existing house is harder than starting from scratch. Matching brick, keeping the building watertight, living on site while we work. We've done it a hundred times. We know how to keep you sane.

  • Single & two-storey extensions
  • Full property renovations
  • Kitchens & bathroom installs
  • Structural steel & knock-throughs
05 / 06

Site Management

Have your own architect and trades, but need someone on the ground running the job? We take a site management appointment and keep everything on programme, on budget and on your side.

  • Programme & sequencing
  • Trade co-ordination
  • Daily H&S & site records
  • Cost reporting to client
06 / 06

Private Client Service

For owners who've never built before and want a discreet, knowledgeable hand. Pre-planning advice, contractor shortlisting, design reviews and site visits. A trusted set of eyes on your project.

  • Pre-planning feasibility
  • Design & contractor reviews
  • Independent site inspections
  • Defects & snagging reports

How We Work

Four stages. No surprises.

The same careful process every time, whether it is a rear extension or a six-bedroom oak-frame country home.

I

Consultation

Paul meets you on site or at home, listens first, asks the right questions. No charge. A first feel for whether we're the right fit.

II

Scope & Quote

Detailed scope of works, fixed pricing where possible, realistic programme. Every line item explained. No hidden assumptions.

III

On Site

Paul runs the job personally. Weekly progress meetings, monthly cost reports, instant answers when something on site needs deciding.

IV

Handover

Snagging, certificates, warranties. We don't leave until you're happy. We've had clients call us back three years later. We still answer.
