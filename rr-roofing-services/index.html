What we do

Every aspect of roofing, covered

Re-Roofs & New Roofs

Full roof strips and replacements, stripped back, re-felted, battened and re-tiled to a watertight finish.

Roof Repairs

Slipped tiles, leaks and storm damage put right fast, with honest advice on whether repair or renewal is best.

Flat Roofing

GRP fibreglass and EPDM rubber systems for extensions, garages, dormers and commercial flat roofs.

Slate & Tile Roofing

Natural slate, clay and concrete tiles laid properly, from period restorations to modern new builds.

Lead Work & Chimneys

Lead flashing, valleys and chimney re-pointing, plus ridge and hip tile re-bedding to keep you weathertight.

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

uPVC fascia and soffit replacement and new guttering, fitted cleanly to move water away from the building.

Commercial & Industrial

Flat and pitched roofing for shops, offices, units and warehouses, with minimal disruption to your business.

Roof Cleaning & Moss

Soft moss removal and treatment to keep tiles clear, protect the roof surface and tidy up the look of the property.

Emergency Call-Outs

Leaking or storm-damaged roof? We make it safe and watertight quickly to stop further damage inside.
