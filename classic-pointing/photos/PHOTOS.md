## Signature "pointing animations" (the client asked for these specifically)
- **Loader**: trowel glides across a channel laying fresh mortar (fill bar) under white-knockout logo.
- **Interactive re-point wall** (`#transform`): TWO stacked canvases — bottom = freshly pointed wall,
  top = worn/dark-jointed wall. An auto "before→after" sweep runs on scroll-in with a dust-extraction
  nozzle; then the user drags cursor/finger to erase (point) the old joints and reveal fresh mortar
  beneath (accumulates permanently via canvas destination-out). "Replay transformation" button re-runs it.
  Fully responsive; touch-enabled.
- Service-card bottom borders animate as a brick/mortar dashed course.
