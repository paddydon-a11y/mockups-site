Our Services

Repointing is all we do, day in day out, so it's done right. Every joint raked out cleanly, every mix colour-matched, every finish weather-tight and built to last.

MOST POPULAR

Brick Repointing

Full and partial repointing of walls, chimneys and elevations, restoring both the look and the weather protection of your brickwork.

Dust Extraction Repointing

Old joints cut out using dust-extraction tools that capture debris at source: a cleaner, safer, low-mess process for your home.

Mortar Joint Removal

Careful raking and grinding out of failed, cracked or cement-hard mortar, back to a sound depth ready for fresh pointing.

Colour & Mortar Matching

How We Work

A clean, careful four-step process.

STEP 01

Rake Out

Old, failed mortar is cut and raked out to a sound depth, ready to take fresh pointing.

STEP 02

Dust Extraction

Extraction tools capture dust and debris at source, protecting you, us and your home.

STEP 03

Repoint

Colour-matched mortar is packed in and finished by hand for a crisp, weather-tight joint.

STEP 04

Cure & Finish

The work is brushed off, cured correctly and left clean. Restored brickwork, built to last.
