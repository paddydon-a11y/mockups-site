About MTN Groundworks

Groundworks done properly

MTN Groundworks Limited is run by Mark Nicolay, handling groundworks and civil engineering for homes and businesses right across Northern Ireland. From dig downs and drainage to demolition, retaining walls, fencing and digger work, we take on the heavy groundwork that gets a site ready and gets it right.

A growing local business built on solid graft — our own plant, an experienced operator on every job, and sites left clean, level and tidy.
