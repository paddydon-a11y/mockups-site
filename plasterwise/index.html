What I Do

A proper trade offering, start to finish.

Damp is my specialism, but Plasterwise covers the full ticket — plastering, painting, building work and external finishes. One number to call, one tradesman accountable.

01

Damp Proofing

Honest diagnosis first — rising, penetrating or condensation. Tanking, chemical DPCs, render systems and cavity drainage where the wall actually needs it.

02

Plastering & Skimming

Flat, polished finishes ready for paint. Full re-skim, patching, ceiling repairs and fresh-board sets. Forty years of trowel time on every wall.

03

General Building

Brickwork, blockwork, openings and structural alterations. Clean job, tidy site, building regs sorted where they're needed.

04

Painting & Decorating

Interior and exterior. Proper prep, two-coat finishes, neat lines on cornices and architraves. Paint that actually lasts a few winters.

05

Extensions

Rear, side and wrap-around extensions taken from foundations through to handover. Coordinated trades, one point of contact.

06

External Finishes

Render, K-rend, pebbledash refresh, and external paint systems. Fronts that look right and shed water properly.
