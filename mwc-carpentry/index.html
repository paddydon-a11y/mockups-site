From the workshop in Masham

MWC Carpentry runs out of Masham, working across the Dales and the Vale of York. We are carpenters first, which means the joinery is square and the second-fix is sharp. Stud walls go up plumb. Doors hang true. Skirting closes on the wall.

Most of our work comes back through people we have already worked for. If you want a Yorkshire trade who turns up, finishes the job, and clears the site, you are in the right place.
