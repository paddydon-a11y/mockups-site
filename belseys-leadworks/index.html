What we do

Specialist leadwork, end to end.

Lead Roofing

Traditional lead sheet to bays, flats, porches and parapets, laid to code and detailed to last generations.

Box Gutters & Valleys

Hand-formed lead box gutters, valley and parapet linings that move with the building and stay watertight.

Chimney Leadwork

Soakers, step and cover flashings, back gutters and full chimney trays to stop leaks at the source.

Standing-Seam & Hard Metal

Standing-seam roofing in lead, zinc and copper, with sharp lines, concealed fixings and a clean modern finish.

Heritage & Conservation

Lead and metalwork for listed buildings, churches and period homes, with sympathetic, like-for-like detail.

Dormers & Bay Roofs

Cheeks, tops and flashings to dormers, bays and porches, clad and dressed for a crisp, lasting seal.

Slate & Tile Roofing

Re-roofs, slipped slates, ridges and repairs finished alongside the leadwork. One team, one trip.

Leak Repairs & Maintenance

Failed flashings, splits and tired lead tracked down and put right before they cost you a ceiling.

Rainwater & Cladding

Lead-welded hoppers, downpipes, ornamental detail and metal cladding to finish the job properly.

Who we are

A new name, with deep roots in the trade.

Belseys Leadworks is a newly established Dorset business built on more than 20 years of hands-on leadwork. From a single slipped flashing to a full lead roof, the work is hand-formed on site, dressed by eye and finished the traditional way.

We work for homeowners, builders and conservation projects across Dorset, turning up when we say, keeping the site tidy, and leaving a roof that's watertight for the long haul. No shortcuts, no call-backs.

How we work

From survey to watertight.

01

Survey & quote
