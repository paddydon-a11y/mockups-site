The Studio

A quiet kind of craft.

Nouvell Decor is a professional building and decorating company trusted by homeowners, landlords and commercial clients across Essex and London — with a reputation built on attention to detail, fair pricing, and finishes that age gracefully.

We work in the houses where the cornice deserves a second coat, where the skirting was probably installed in 1904, and where speed is never a reason to skip the preparation. That's where we're at our best.

Every project begins with a walkthrough and a conversation. We agree the scope, the colours, the programme — and then we stick to it. Fixed prices. No surprises. Friendly team on hand, proper aftercare, and a signed-off job at the end.

Whether it's a single room, a full townhouse, or an office refurbishment — we'd love to quote for it.

III.
Our Approach

Prepared well.
Painted properly.
Signed off.

Our work is grounded in a simple belief: the thing that separates a good finish from a great one is almost always what happened before the paint went on.

We fill, we sand, we prime. We protect your floors and furniture. We tidy every night. And when it's time to hand the keys back, it's handed back better than we found it.

  • i. Walkthrough & fixed quotation
  • ii. Colour consultation & sampling
  • iii. Full preparation & protection
  • iv. Careful application, two coats
  • v. Final snag & aftercare
