Who we are

Fencing built for security and scale

Riverside Fencing Contractors are a Midlands fencing outfit focused on commercial and security work. We run as a trusted subcontractor on highly specialised secure units for NHS mental health facilities, alongside the full range of perimeter, sports and access fencing.

From a single hoarding line to a fully secured site, we turn up, work clean and hand over to spec. Domestic fencing and landscaping rounds out what we do.

  • Specialist secure-unit experience (NHS subcontractor)
  • Mesh, palisade, hoarding, Heras, bollards and MUGA
  • Working with main contractors across the Midlands
