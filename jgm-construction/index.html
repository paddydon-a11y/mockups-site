03 / The firm

One team for the whole build

JGM Construction is a London building company that takes a project all the way, from a house built up out of the ground to a tired property brought back to life inside and out.

Because we cover the building work alongside plastering, electrics, plumbing and kitchens, you deal with one team and one standard rather than juggling separate trades. Everything is planned, priced and managed from the first visit through to handover.

Based in Wembley, we work across London and travel further afield, including Birmingham and Leicester, for larger projects.
