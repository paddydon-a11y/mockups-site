What we build

New homes, lodges and everything in between.

CM Building handles the full build, from plot to handover. Five core services, all delivered directly by Charlie and the same team throughout.

01 / New Build Houses

New build homes from the ground up

Full new build projects across Derby and Derbyshire. Charlie runs groundworks, structure, roofing, first and second fix through to a fully finished handover. One team, one programme, one number to call.

  • Groundworks
  • Brickwork
  • Roof structure
  • First fix
  • Second fix
  • Finishing
02 / Lodges and Cabins

Bespoke lodges and timber-frame cabins

Purpose-built lodges for residential plots, holiday sites and rural land. Timber frame, log construction or hybrid build, designed to suit the site and signed off to building regulations.

  • Timber frame
  • Log construction
  • Insulation
  • Decking
  • Utilities
03 / Extensions

Single and double-storey extensions

Side returns, rear extensions and two-storey side builds. From building regs to final coat, managed start to finish.

04 / Renovations

Full home renovations

Strip-out, structural work, replumb, rewire, kitchen, bathrooms and decoration. Old houses are no problem.

05 / Groundworks

Groundworks and site preparation

Foundations, drainage, concrete slabs and site prep for new builds and extensions across the East Midlands.

About CM Building

Run by a builder, not a project manager.

Charlie Mee has been on building sites for over fifteen years. CM Building started in Derby and has grown into a trusted name for new builds, lodges and extensions across Derbyshire and the East Midlands.

Charlie is on every job from day one to handover. He doesn't pass it on to a foreman and walk away. That's why the quality stays consistent and the dates get kept.
