About C.J.L

A painter with a professional touch.

C.J.L Decorating is run by Callum Littlewood, a Cambridgeshire painter & decorator with twelve years on the brush. Based out of March, covering Wisbech, Ely, Chatteris, Whittlesey and surrounding fenland villages.

Every job starts the same way: dust sheets down, surfaces prepped, edges cut in sharp, and walls finished with two clean coats. No splatter, no rushed touch-ups, no walking off mid-job.
