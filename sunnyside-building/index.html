About Sunnyside

Proper builders for proper homes.

Sunnyside was founded by Ermal Shehu — a builder who'd had enough of jobs finished badly and clients left in the lurch. The idea was simple: do it right, be where you said you'd be, and take pride in the finish.

We handle the full scope of residential building across Shropshire and Birmingham. Extensions, full renovations, loft conversions, structural foundations, roofing — including EPDM flat roofs — driveways, landscaping, and the kind of careful repointing that makes a period house look right again.

We don't take on more than we can finish well. Most of our work comes through word of mouth, and we'd like it to stay that way.

Ermal Shehu
Founder · Lead Builder

How we work

A process that respects your home.

Four stages. Agreed in writing before we start. No surprise invoices, no mystery delays, no "we'll sort it later."

01

Consultation

Site visit, measure up, understand the brief. We'll talk honestly about what's possible, what it'll cost, and what we'd do differently.

02

Quote & Schedule

Line-by-line fixed quote with a proper programme of works. Every stage, every material, every milestone laid out.

03

Build

We turn up when we said we would. Weekly updates, photos, and a clean site at the end of every day. Changes signed off in writing.

04

Handover

Snag walk-through, building-control paperwork, warranties explained. We only leave when you're genuinely happy.
