What We Do

Welding and fabrication, done to spec.

From CNC plasma cutting to coded pipe welding, bespoke gates to one-off custom builds. Mild steel, stainless, aluminium and galvanised, on site or in the workshop.

CNC Plasma Cutting

Precision plasma profiling of plate and sheet. Brackets, gussets, signage and custom parts cut clean to your drawings or DXF files.

Coded Pipe Welding

Coded root and cap on carbon and stainless pipe. Spool fabrication, process and site pipework welded to the standard the job demands.

General Metal Work

Repairs, modifications, brackets and steelwork in mild steel, stainless, aluminium and galvanised. No job too small.

On-Site Welding

Mobile welding rig and generator, full PPE and safe systems of work. Welding and repairs carried out where the work is.

Bespoke Gates & Railings

Made-to-measure gates, railings, balustrade and security bars. Fabricated, galvanised or powder-coated and fitted to suit.

Custom Fabrication

Staircases, platforms, frames, cages and one-off builds. Bring a drawing or a back-of-an-envelope sketch and we will make it.

About HAZFAB

Welding and fabrication, based on Red Marsh Industrial Estate.

HAZFAB LTD is a welding and fabrication business on Red Marsh Industrial Estate in Thornton-Cleveleys. We take on everything from CNC plasma cutting and coded pipe welding to bespoke gates, railings, staircases and structural steel.

Whether it is a one-off repair, a set of made-to-measure gates or a full fabrication job to drawing, the work is cut clean, welded to spec and finished to last. On site across the Fylde coast and Lancashire, or in the workshop.

  • CNC plasma cutting, coded pipe welding and general metal work.
  • Bespoke gates, railings, staircases and custom fabrication.
  • Mild steel, stainless, aluminium and galvanised, on site or in the shop.
