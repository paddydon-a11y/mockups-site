Cladding & Roof Coatings

Industrial unit recoats inside and out. Profile cladding, standing seam, single-ply. Pressure wash, prime, two-coat polyurethane finish. No rip-out, fraction of a re-sheet.

Roller Shutters

Faded, rust-bloomed or just rebranded shutters re-sprayed in trade colour. Done in-situ on site, often inside a single shutdown window. RAL or BS spec to your colour standard.

Brief us Hospitality

Hotel Bedroom Respray

Fitted wardrobes, headboards, vanities, doors. Hotel chains and B&Bs. Worked floor-by-floor against the room schedule with overnight cure. No replacement, no skip hire.

Brief us Compliance

Internal Fire Doors

Fire door re-spray to manufacturer's stated finish. Hotels, HMOs, public buildings. Fast turnaround, low-VOC option for occupied buildings, certs back on file for the FM pack.

Bath, Sink & Shower Re-enamel

Hotel bathroom rooms turned around in a day. Re-enamel chipped baths, sinks and shower trays. Two-pack high-build top-coat, bonded primer, ready for guests in 24 hours.

Granite & Resin Over Tiles

Kitchens, bathrooms, splashbacks. Sprayed granite or resin finish straight over existing tiles. No rip-out, seamless, dry overnight. Hotel and rental refresh on a tight budget.

Brief us Public

School & Public Steelwork

School railings, bike-shed structures, balustrades, sports cages. Stripped, primed, polyurethane top-coat in a school-holiday window. CRB-cleared technicians where the brief asks.

About ICED

The choice for homes & businesses.

ICED Spray Coatings boost durability, resist wear and protect surfaces with sleek, lasting finishes. Reliable solutions for domestic and commercial needs alike. Proud ProPerla approved applicators for breathable, weather-resistant external wall coatings.

From single-home render to dealership facades, warehouse cladding, louvre doors and structural steel, the spec stays the same: trained spray technicians, RAMS and COSHH on every site, method statements signed off before we step on. Northampton base, working nationwide.
