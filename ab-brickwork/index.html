What we do

Brickwork,
start to finish.

Five things on the menu. We keep it tight on purpose. Same standard whether it's a single repointing job or a two-storey extension built off architect's drawings.

01

Brickwork

Garden walls, boundary walls, brick face on extensions and new builds. Foundations dug to depth, DPC laid, courses lined off a string, weep holes set.

BoundaryRetainingBrick faceSoldier course
02

Blockwork

Inner skin on cavity builds, foundation blocks, dense or thermal block. Cavity insulation set as we go. Levelled and gauged, ready for plaster or face.

CavityThermalFoundationsInner skin
03

Extensions

Single and double-storey rear extensions, side returns, infills. Working off architect's plans and Building Regs. Brick matched to the existing house.

RearSide returnTwo-storeyBrick match

New Builds

Full new-build brick and block work for developers and self-builders. From slab to roofline, brick face to detail courses. Trade rates available.

Self-buildDeveloperTrade ratesSpec brick

Raked out by hand to depth, cleaned, then repointed with a lime or sand-cement mix matched to the original. Weatherstruck, bucket-handle or flush. Period properties a speciality.

About us

Two trowels.
One company.

A&B Brickwork Ltd is a small North London brickwork outfit. We're on every job from the first dig to the last brush. Brick face that matches the house, courses that come off a string line, and a tidy site at the end of the day.

We work for homeowners, builders and architects across the city. Domestic repairs through to full new builds. Same level of care whether it's a wall in a garden or a two-storey rear extension.
