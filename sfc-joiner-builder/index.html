Who we are

One team, trade to trim.

S.F.C is a Kilwinning joiner and builder covering all aspects of joinery and general building. Because the same outfit handles the structure and the fit-out, the detail is right and the timber work is finished to a joiner's standard, not rushed at the end.

New builds, extensions and renovations through to bespoke fitted furniture and commercial fit-outs. Free quotes, tidy sites, and work we are happy to put our name on.
