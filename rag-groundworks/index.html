About us

Cornish hands.
Honest groundwork.

R A Groundworks & Utilities Ltd is a small, family-run outfit based in Treviscoe, St Austell. Run by Robert Astin, we cover the whole of Cornwall and most of Devon, taking on everything from a back-garden patio to full foundations and drainage on a new build.

We turn up when we say, work clean, and finish what we start. No subbing the job out, no surprise bills, no leaving site untidy on the way out. The same lads you meet on day one are finishing the last edge.
