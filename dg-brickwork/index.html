What We Build

Brick by brick. Every job, every brief.

From a single course of repointing to the full masonry shell of a new-build house. Direct for homeowners, alongside main contractors and architects.

Brickwork & Blockwork

All standard masonry, internal blockwork, external skin work, garden walls, piers and feature brickwork. Whatever the brick spec on the drawing, we'll match it.

New Builds

Full masonry shells for new-build homes, working from architect's drawings or a main contractor's package. Foundations to wallplate.

Extensions

Single and double-storey rear, side and wrap-around extensions. We'll match into the existing house, awkward bricks and unusual bonds included.

Alterations

Knock-throughs, opening-ups, internal walls removed and made good. Always tied in properly with the original structure.

Repointing

Lime and cement repointing, period-correct mortar mixes, weathered struck or flush joints. Done so it doesn't look new for the sake of it.

Heritage Work

Sensitive restoration on listed and period properties. Reclaimed bricks, traditional bonds, lime mortar and consideration for the building's age.

Retaining & Boundary Walls

Engineered retaining walls, garden walls, piers and gateposts, copings and engineering bricks where the loads demand it.

Structural Work & Steels

Padstones, RSJ installation, structural openings, lintels and load-bearing tie-ins. Working to engineer's drawings throughout.

About DG Brickwork

Time-served bricklayers. Proper masonry standard.

DG Brickwork & Building is a Canterbury-based masonry contractor working across Kent, specialising in all aspects of brickwork and general building.

From a traditional repointed boundary wall to a full new-build shell, the standard is the same: clean lines, the right brick, the right mortar, and joints you'd be happy to live with for the next sixty years.

  • Direct for homeowners on extensions and alterations, alongside builders and architects on bigger projects.
  • Heritage and listed work taken seriously: lime mortar, reclaimed brick, period-correct joints.
  • Fully insured, fair pricing, honest timescales. Site kept clean, materials respected.
