A trade worth doing properly.

DG Brickwork & Building is a Canterbury-based masonry outfit covering all aspects of brickwork and general building across Kent.

From a traditional repointed boundary wall to a full new-build shell, the standard is the same: clean lines, the right brick, the right mortar, and joints you'd be happy to live with for the next sixty years.

We work direct for homeowners on extensions and alterations, alongside builders and architects on structural steels and heritage projects, and on our own restoration work where the brief is to put a building back the way it was meant to look.

/ Heritage & restoration

Old buildings, built right.

Period brickwork doesn't forgive shortcuts. The wrong mortar mix can hold moisture in soft Victorian brick and crumble it from the inside. The wrong bond on a repair sticks out for the next century.

We do heritage work the way it was originally done: lime mortars where the building needs to breathe, period-correct joints, reclaimed bricks where matches matter, and a slower pace where the job demands it.

  • Lime mortar repointing
  • Reclaimed brick matching
  • Listed building work
  • Chimney rebuilds
  • Tudor-revival & Edwardian
  • Boundary & estate walls
