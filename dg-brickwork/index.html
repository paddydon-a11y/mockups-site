/ Heritage & restoration

Old buildings, built right.

Period brickwork doesn't forgive shortcuts. The wrong mortar mix can hold moisture in soft Victorian brick and crumble it from the inside. The wrong bond on a repair sticks out for the next century.

We do heritage work the way it was originally done: lime mortars where the building needs to breathe, period-correct joints, reclaimed bricks where matches matter, and a slower pace where the job demands it.

  • Lime mortar repointing
  • Reclaimed brick matching
  • Listed building work
  • Chimney rebuilds
  • Tudor-revival & Edwardian
  • Boundary & estate walls
