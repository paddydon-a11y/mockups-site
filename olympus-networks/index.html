What we install, terminate and test.

01 · copper

Cat6 / Cat6a installation

Floor, ceiling, riser and structured cabling. Pulled, dressed, terminated and channel tested to spec.

02 · fibre

Fibre optic splicing

Single-mode and multi-mode terminations. Fusion splicing, OTDR traces and signed PDF reports on completion.

03 · cctv

IP camera installs

4K IP and analogue cameras. Discreet routing, NVR and remote view setup. Recorded, tagged and labelled.

04 · wireless

Wireless access points

Cisco, Ubiquiti and Aruba kit. Coverage survey, install, configure, label. Mesh and roaming sorted.

05 · racks

Patch panels & termination

RJ45, LC and SC. Dressed bundles, labelled cables, clean racks. End-to-end IDs documented for the handover pack.

06 · containment

Small containment

Basket, tray, trunking and conduit. Installed flush to walls, painted in if needed. Cable routes that disappear.
