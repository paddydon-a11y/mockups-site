About ADB

Built around your home

ADB Building Services is run by Anthony de Burgh out of Torbay, working across South Hams, Exeter, Teignbridge and the wider South Devon coast.

We take on the full build on period properties and modern homes: structural extensions, refurbishments, conversions, kitchens and bathrooms. Every trade coordinated, one point of contact, no subcontractor merry-go-round.
