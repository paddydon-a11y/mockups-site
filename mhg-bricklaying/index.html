01 / Brickwork

Brickwork & Blockwork

Cavity walls, single skin, blockwork and foundations. Laid level, jointed clean, built to line.

02 / Extensions

Extensions

Single and double-storey. Footings, brick skin and blockwork through to roof plate, start to finish.

03 / Repointing

Repointing

Rake out tired joints and repoint to match. Brings old brickwork back to life and keeps the weather out.

04 / Concreting

Concreting

Footings, bases, slabs and oversites. Shuttered, poured and floated to a solid, level finish.

05 / Outside

Patios & Driveways

Block paving, slabs and porcelain. Proper sub-base, edge restraints, laid right the first time.

06 / Gardens

Landscaping

07 / Building

Repairs & General Building Work

Brick repairs, lintels, structural openings, knock-throughs and the snags other trades leave behind. If it needs a builder, we cover it.
