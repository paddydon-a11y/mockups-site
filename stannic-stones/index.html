About Stannic

A small London outfit. Big on finish.

Stannic Stones is Mohanad and a small fitting team. We work in marble, quartz and granite. Templating, cutting, polishing and fitting in-house so the finish is ours, end to end.

Most of what we do is kitchens. Islands, runs, splashbacks, the lot. We also handle bathrooms, fireplaces and the odd commercial fit. Based in London, on site anywhere within 50 miles.
