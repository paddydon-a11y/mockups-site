Specialist Work

The jobs other trades
quietly turn down.

Three areas we've been called in for, again and again, for 30 years. If another plasterer has told you it's "too messy" or "not worth the hassle" — give us a ring.

Artex Removal

Those old textured ceilings scraped off, encapsulated where needed, and re-skimmed dead flat. Proper dust sheets, zero drama.

Damp Proofing

Penetrating damp, rising damp and salt-bloom — cause diagnosed, treated, and re-plastered with a proper salt-retardant system.

Fireplaces

Old fireplace openings exposed, made-good, lime-plastered or rendered around solid-fuel installs. Hearths in, chimneys breasted cleanly.

About Us

A family on the
trowel, since 1994.

IC Plasterers is a family-run plastering and building company covering Edinburgh, the Lothians, Fife and the Scottish Borders. John's been skimming walls and rendering gable ends for more than 30 years — and these days David's out on the jobs too, with all trades backed up behind them.

Our aim is customer satisfaction on every single job — from a patched-up ceiling to a full house strip-and-render. We quote fair, we turn up when we say we will, and we leave the site tidy.
