Monifieth · Dundee · Angus

Homes by Dominique is the current chapter of a design-and-build firm that has been working Angus homes since 1993. Three generations, one architectural eye, and a trades team kept in-house. We draw what we build, we build what we draw, and we answer for both.

Our remit is residential: considered kitchens, disciplined bathrooms, sensitive extensions and complete renovations. Occasionally, we take a commercial brief — a hotel, a care home, a portfolio of lettings — when the work warrants the same care as a private house.

The house always sets the brief. We listen first, draw second, and commit once.

Dominique McIntosh Director & Design Lead
