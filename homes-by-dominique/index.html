Our story

Dublin-born, Dundee-made,
Home-obsessed.

Dominique McIntosh is the heart and soul of Homes by Dominique. A qualified architectural consultant, a mother of three, and the third iteration of a family business that's been fitting Scottish homes since 1993.

We're not just designers. We're a full design-and-build studio, which is the difference between a Pinterest board and a kitchen you can actually cook in. Our joiners, tilers, plumbers and electricians work under one roof, to one plan, for one price.

We draw it, we source it, we build it, and we clean up behind ourselves. When we hand you the keys, every drawer closes properly, every grout line is straight, and every switch works the first time.

Dominique Owner & Architectural Consultant

The HbD Journey

Three steps. One team.

The strength of design-and-build is that you don't hand off between contractors. The drawing, the materials and the install are all us, in that order.

Design

A home visit, a full survey, and an architectural drawing set that accounts for the plumbing runs before the tiles go down.

Supply

Trade accounts across the best British and German brands. You get the quality, we pass on the price. No markup games.

Install

Our own joiners, tilers, plumbers, electricians and decorators. One programme, one site manager, one person accountable from first day to handover.
