Chapter 01 — The Studio

A family of architects, builders & fitters working under one roof.

Homes By Dominique is an all-trades home development contractor based in Monifieth, Angus. Since 1993 we've been building, extending and refurbishing homes across Tayside — with the architect, the designer and the tradesmen all on the same team.

From a small bathroom rework to a full ground-up extension, you get one brief, one price, one point of contact. We draw it, cost it, build it, finish it — and we're still there twelve months later when you ring with a question about the boiler.

Chapter 05 — How We Work

Four chapters. One studio.

From the first call to the twelve-month check-in, the rhythm is the same — transparent, measured, written down.

01.

Meet & measure.

A free studio visit or on-site meeting. We listen to what the home needs to do — for now and ten years from now.

02.

Design & cost.

Concept, technical drawings, material choices and a clear fixed quote. You see the whole picture before anything moves.

03.

Build & finish.

One team on site. Weekly updates, tidy boundaries, finishes protected. We do the small bits right, not just the big bits.

04.

Handover & stay.

Snag walk, keys, warranty pack. A year later we ring you. We're still here in Monifieth — the relationship doesn't end on the invoice.
