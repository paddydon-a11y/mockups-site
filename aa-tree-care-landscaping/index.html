What we do

Trees, lawns, paving and everything between.

Tree surgery

Crown reductions, deadwooding, felling and dismantles. All access methods, anything from a back-garden cherry to mature oaks.

All aspects of paving

Indian sandstone, porcelain, block paving, granite setts. Properly dug out, sub-base laid, jointed and sealed.

Hard landscaping

Retaining walls, raised beds, steps, sleeper edging, gravel beds. Levels worked out first, finished to last decades.

Turfing & lawns

New lawns from scratch: rotovated, levelled, top soil down, premium turf. Old lawns lifted and replaced where the ground’s gone.

Fencing

Closeboard, featheredge, hit-and-miss, panels and posts. Concrete-set, properly aligned, treated. Replacements after storms too.

Garden maintenance

Regular and one-off cuts. Lawn mowing, edging, weeding, borders kept, leaves cleared. Monthly contracts available.
