What we do

Hard landscaping & groundworks done right

From the dig and the sub-base to the final course of paving, TB Construction handles the whole job in-house — so your driveway, patio or wall is built on solid ground and finished to last.

Driveways

Block paving, natural stone and tarmac driveways laid over a properly dug, compacted sub-base — with the right falls, drainage and edging to keep them looking sharp for years.

Patios

Indian sandstone, riven and sawn stone patios, steps and seating areas — laid full-bed and pointed to a clean, lasting finish.

Porcelain Patios

Premium porcelain paving for a crisp, contemporary finish — low-maintenance, slip-resistant and laid dead level.

Groundworks

Excavation, sub-bases, drainage, muck-away and levelling — the groundwork that everything else is built on.

Digger Work

Mini and midi diggers with an experienced operator for excavation, footings, dig-outs and site clearance.

Garden Walls

Brick and block garden walls, retaining walls and piers — built plumb with neat coursing and quality copings.

Tarmac

Tarmac driveways, hardstandings and access — laid hot and rolled for a smooth, hard-wearing surface.

Shed Bases

Concrete and paved bases for sheds, cabins and hot tubs — dead level and built to take the weight.

Footings

Excavated and poured footings and foundations for extensions, walls and outbuildings — set out square and to depth.
