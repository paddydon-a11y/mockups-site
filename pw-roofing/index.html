What We Do

What We Do

Re-roofs, repairs, lead work and roofline. Covering the full spread of domestic roofing.

01

Re-roofs

Full strip and retile on terraces, semis and new builds. New battens, breathable membrane, concrete tile or natural slate.

02

Repairs

Slipped tiles, leaks, blown ridges and storm damage. Same-week call-outs across South Wales.

03

Lead & chimneys

Lead flashing, valleys, soakers and chimney rebuilds. Code 4 and code 5 lead.

04

Roofline & moss

Fascias, soffits, guttering and moss treatments. Keeps the slate breathing and the water moving.
