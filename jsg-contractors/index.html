New Roofs

Strip and recover with new battens, breathable membrane and your choice of tile. Built to last decades.

02

Flat Roofs

New flat roofs in EPDM rubber and GRP fibreglass. Extensions, garages, dormers and bay windows.

Full House Re-Roofs

Chimney Repointing

Chimney stacks raked out, repointed, capped and made watertight. Stops leaks at the most exposed point.
