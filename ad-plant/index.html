What we do in the dirt.

Groundworks

Site strip, dig out, oversite, drains and services trenches. Mini-digger or 6-tonne, your choice.

Foundations to DPC

Strip, trench-fill, raft. Set out, dug, poured and built up to damp-proof course ready for trades.

Concrete slabs

Power-floated, brushed, tamped. Reinforced or fibre, indoor or out. Pumped where needed.

Sewage treatment plants

Klargester, Marsh and Graf installs. Sized to property, dug, set, plumbed and commissioned.

Drainage

Foul, surface, French drains and soakaways. Run, jointed and tested before backfill.

Concrete crushing

Old slabs, hardcore, broken-out concrete. Crushed to MOT or 6F2 spec on your site or ours.

Earthworks & clearance

Cut and fill, bulk dig, muckaway. Site clearance to a level, ready for the next trade.

Retaining walls

Stone, block or gabion. Founded properly, drained behind, finished to match the site.
