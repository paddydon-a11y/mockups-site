Who we help

Young adults the system has finished with.

We work with people aged 18 to 30 leaving prison, sleeping rough, or coming out of acute mental health care. Most have nothing to land on. We give them a real bedroom, a key, a key worker, and time.

Reachable is funded by a mix of local authority placements and direct public donations. Every pound goes into the house, the people, or the next refurb.

01

Mental health recovery

Step-down accommodation after acute care, with mentoring and routine.

02

Leaving prison

Through-the-gate housing for young adults with no address to return to.

03

Homelessness

Off the street into a shared house with a plan for the next twelve months.

04

Pathway to move-on

Budgeting, cooking, work-readiness, and help into permanent tenancies.
