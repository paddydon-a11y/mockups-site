Billy McLoughlin Founder · Fabricator

We don't sell off-the-shelf kits. Every job is drawn for the room it's going in, cut to the millimetre, welded and finished in the workshop, and fitted on site by the same hands that made it.

Glazed Forge North started in 2014 with a single MIG welder, a tape measure and a brief from a friend who needed a steel-framed door for his kitchen. Twelve years later we're still drawing every job from scratch, still finishing every weld in-house, and still answering the phone ourselves.

If you want a fabricator who'll show up, listen properly to what the room is asking for, and tell you the truth about what's possible inside the budget, that's the brief. Affordable, talented, honest. We'll bring the biscuits.
