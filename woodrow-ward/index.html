What we do

Brickwork,
start to finish.

Five services, kept tight on purpose. Same standard whether it is a single repointing job or a two-storey extension built off architect's drawings.

01

Brickwork

Garden walls, boundary walls, brick face on extensions and new builds. Foundations dug to depth, DPC laid, courses lined off a string, weep holes set where needed.

BoundaryRetainingBrick faceSoldier course
02

Blockwork

Inner skin on cavity builds, foundation blocks, dense or thermal block. Cavity insulation set as we go. Levelled and gauged, ready for plaster or face.

CavityThermalFoundationsInner skin
03

Extensions

Single and double-storey rear extensions, side returns, infills. Working off architect's plans and Building Regs. Brick matched to the existing house.

RearSide returnTwo-storeyBrick match
04

New Builds

Full new-build brick and blockwork for developers and self-builders. From slab to roofline, brick face to detail courses. Trade rates available.

Self-buildDeveloperTrade ratesSpec brick
05

Pointing and Repointing

Raked out by hand to depth, cleaned, then repointed with a lime or sand-cement mix matched to the original. Weatherstruck, bucket-handle or flush finish. Period properties a speciality.

Lime mortarSand-cementWeatherstruckBucket-handlePeriod
