What we do

Every job up there. One trusted team.

New Roofs

Strip, batten, breathable membrane, tile or slate. Pitched or hipped, sized right to last 30+ years.

10-yr guarantee

Roof Repairs

Slipped tiles, broken slate, lifted ridge, valley leaks. Diagnosed up close, fixed at the cause, not patched.

Emergency Callouts 24/7

Storm damage, sudden leak, slipped tile after a gale. Tarpaulin on the same day, permanent fix scheduled.

Flat Roofs

Single-ply EPDM, fibreglass GRP and felt systems. 20-year membrane life and clean lap details.

Lead Work

Chimneys, valleys, dormers and flashings. Code 4 / code 5 lead, dressed and bossed by hand.

Roof Windows & Dormers

Velux, Fakro and bespoke dormer cheeks. Sized for the rafter spacing, weather-flashed in lead.
