What we do

Three jobs.
Done end to end.

Full builds, extensions and renovations. Run end-to-end across the Staffordshire patch with the same trades and the same bloke on site every day. Tell us about your project.

01 / Full Builds

Plot to finished house.

From a clear plot to a house you can move into. Groundworks, foundations, frame, roof, first and second fix, finishes. We don't sub the bits that matter, so the standard stays consistent.

  • Groundworks & foundations
  • Brick, block & render
  • Roof structure & tiling
  • First & second fix
  • Finishes & handover
02 / Extensions

Single, double or wraparound.

Side returns, rear extensions, double-storey side builds, garage conversions and full wraparounds. Drawings to handover, structural sign-off included, finished and decorated ready to use.

  • Single-storey rear & side
  • Double-storey extensions
  • Wraparounds
  • Garage conversions
  • Outbuildings & annexes
03 / Renovations

Full home refurbishment.

Strip-out, structural alterations, replumb and rewire, plastering, joinery, kitchens and bathrooms. Period property is bread and butter for us. Listed and conservation work we're set up for.

  • Kitchens & bathrooms
  • Period & listed work

How we work

Four stages.
No surprises.

From first call to last snag, the same outfit walks the project through. You'll deal with the same bloke from the on-site visit to the keys back.

Step 01

Site visit & quote

Come out, walk the site, listen to what you actually want. Free, no commitment either way. A written quote follows inside the week.

Step 02

Drawings & planning

Architect drawings, planning and building regs through known local people we've worked with for years. Listed and conservation handled.

Step 03

Build & manage

Programmed, costed, managed by the same outfit. Same trades on site every day, same standard, same answer when you ring up.

Step 04

Handover & aftercare

Snagged, cleaned, walked round and signed off. Anything that needs coming back for, we come back for. That's just the way it works.
