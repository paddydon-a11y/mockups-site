What We Do

Six electrical disciplines, under one contractor.

Each discipline staffed by time-served electricians, supervised by project management, and completed under Curran's own supervision from first strike to final test certificate.

01.

Commercial Installation

Office fit-outs, hospitality projects, retail units and mixed-use developments. Lighting design, containment, distribution and data, delivered on programme.

02.

Industrial Installation

Three-phase installations, factory rewires, plant supplies and switchgear for manufacturing and logistics sites across the North West.

03.

Listed & Heritage

Sympathetic electrical works to listed buildings, heritage venues and period property, preserving architectural detail while meeting modern standards.

04.

Residential Installation

New builds, full refurbishments and apartment blocks, wired to spec with attention to finish, convenience and the homeowner's brief.

05.

Inspection & Testing

EICR reports, landlord certificates, periodic inspections and PAT testing for commercial and residential clients, with clear reporting and follow-up.

06.

Design & Consultancy

Early-stage electrical design, load calculations, lighting schemes and specification writing for architects, developers and main contractors.

The Firm

A Manchester electrical contractor, family run since 1988.

Curran Contracting Services Ltd has delivered electrical works across the North of England for three and a half decades. From single-day fault diagnosis to multi-phase hotel refurbishments, the team takes on work at every scale.

The firm is run day-to-day by Gary Curran with Julie Curran handling office administration from the Wythenshawe head office. Every project is supervised by a time-served electrician, every certificate signed off in person.
