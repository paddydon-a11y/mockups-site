How We Work

One contractor. One conversation.

Design and build, properly done, means the client has a single point of accountability. Curran handles the design brief, the build programme, the trades and the electrical spec, all under one roof.

01.

Full Refurbishment

Ground floor through to loft: structural alterations, plastering, joinery, decoration, flooring, kitchens, bathrooms and of course the complete electrical rewire, managed as one project.

02.

Residential Fit-Out

Penthouses, apartments and period property fit-outs. Bespoke joinery, finish carpentry, tiling and trim, finished to a standard suitable for landmark addresses.

03.

Landlord & Investor Projects

Block refurbishments, HMO conversions and pre-sale refresh for landlords, developers and property investors across Manchester, Cheshire and Lancashire.

04.

Heritage & Listed

Sympathetic design and build works to period and listed property. Finishes specified with the building in mind, consents handled, site protocols observed.

The Approach

Two generations of builders & electricians under one roof.

Curran Contracting Services has quietly delivered residential design and build projects across the North of England for over three decades. The firm's advantage is simple: in-house electrical capability means a single contractor can take a project from structural opening-out to fully certified handover.

Each project is led personally by Gary Curran. Scope, programme and cost are agreed on paper before the first skip arrives. No surprises, no running bills, no handover delays.
