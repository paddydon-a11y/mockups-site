About AJM

Old-fashioned craft, made for modern homes.

AJM Bespoke Joinery is a Northumberland workshop making fitted furniture the way it should be made, drawn for your space, built from solid materials and finished by hand.

We measure it, make it and fit it ourselves, so the piece that goes in is exactly the piece we drew.

From a run of wardrobes to a whole library, you deal with the maker from first sketch to final coat of paint. Tidy on site, honest on price, and not finished until it is right.
