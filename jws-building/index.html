Every trade, one outfit.

No agency-marked-up subbies, no chasing a different number for every stage. We run the whole job and our reputation walks home with us.

Brickwork & blockwork

Face brickwork, blockwork, retaining walls, reveals, arches and feature courses. Neat joints, clean mortar lines, tidy site at end of day.

01 / Service

New builds & extensions

From slab to roofline. Single and two-storey extensions, garden rooms, garage conversions and full ground-up builds.

02 / Service

Renovations

Full property refurbs, structural openings, knock-throughs, period restoration. We sequence the trades so the job actually finishes.

03 / Service

Patios & paving

Indian stone, porcelain, travertine, slate or sandstone. Set on a proper mortar bed, pointed, sealed and left clean.

04 / Service

Landscaping & gardens

Levels, drainage, edging, lawns, planting beds, fencing and the lot. Garden engineered to drain, not just laid.

05 / Service

Stone masonry

Period stonework, copings, ball caps, pillars and feature walls. Hand-finished to match the original where it matters.

06 / Service

Plastering

Skim, hardwall, bonding and patch work. Walls ready for paint, not ready for filler. Sharp arrises, flat finish.

07 / Service

Joinery

First and second-fix. Door hanging, skirtings, picture rails, fitted shelving, kitchens carcased and worktops set right.
