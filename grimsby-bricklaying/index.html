What we do

All types of brickwork & general building.

From a single garden wall to a full house extension, it is the same team on the tools and the same standard of work. Here is what we are called out for most.

01

Brickwork & Blockwork

Facing brick, blockwork, rebuilds, repairs and re-pointing. Clean coursing, neat joints and a finish that lifts the whole property, whatever the brick.

  • Rendering prep
  • Re-pointing

Project Management
