What we do

Groundworks, from the first dig to DPC

Footings & Foundations

Trench-fill, strip and raft foundations dug, poured and built up to damp-proof course, set out to your drawings and levels.

Substructure Brickwork

Facing brick and blockwork up to DPC, levelled, bonded and ready for the build above.

Drainage

Foul and surface water runs, manholes, gullies, ducting and connections.

Concrete & Slabs

Oversite, reinforced slabs, beam-and-block floors and hardstandings.

Dig & Muck Away

Reduced dig, setting out, levels and site clearance for new plots.

Extensions & Outbuildings

Footings, bases and brickwork for extensions, garages and outbuildings, on domestic and commercial projects alike.

Who we are

A groundworks outfit you can build on

Cozma MD is run by Cristian Cozma, working across Northamptonshire on new-build developments and private projects. From the first dig to DPC, we set out, excavate, lay footings, build the substructure brickwork and get the drainage in, ready for the build above.

Carded, insured and reliable, with levels and bonding done right, it is a name developers and homeowners come back to.
