What We Do

Full drylining & plastering package.

A complete internal package for main contractors. From the structural metal framing through to a skimmed, taped and decoration-ready finish. One trade, one programme, one point of contact.

01 — Core Trade

Metal Stud Partitions

Steel-framed partitions and shaft walls built to drawing: single, double or staggered stud, lined to acoustic and fire spec. Set out true, fixed plumb, ready to board.

About P&N

A drylining firm that turns up.

P&N Contracts Ltd is a commercial drylining and plastering contractor based in Newry, working right across Northern Ireland, the Republic and over on the UK mainland. We handle the full internal package — metal stud, SFS, boarding, ceilings, insulation and finishing.

We work the way main contractors need us to: priced properly, programmed tight, resourced to hit the dates and left clean for the next trade. From a single block to a full development, the standard is the same on every wall.
