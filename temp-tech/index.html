What Bill Does

From a missing tile to a full strip and re-tile. All domestic roofing work, carried out personally by Bill.

Re-Roofing & Repairs

Full strip and re-tile, missing or slipped tile fixes, and storm damage repair. Done properly the first time.

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

Full roofline replacement in uPVC. Rotten timbers stripped out, new boards and seamless guttering fitted clean.

Chimney Repair & Removal

Chimney stack repointing, removal and capping. Lead flashing renewed, all roof work made good behind.

Flat Roofing

Felt, EPDM and GRP flat roofing for extensions, garages, dormers and bays. Watertight finish, fitted to last.
