Roof repairs & replacements

02
Storm & weather damage
Emergency tarping, temporary covers, and written insurance reports. Most storm jobs watertight inside 24 hours.

EPDM rubber flat roofs

GRP fibreglass flat roofs

Torch-on felt roofing
Traditional multi layer felt with clean drip details and neat trims. A proven system at an honest price.

Meet Tommy

A family-run Widnes firm, built on word of mouth.

Home N Dry has been looking after roofs and properties across Widnes, Runcorn and Warrington for over two decades. What started as a one man roofing outfit has grown, honestly and slowly, into a small trusted team. All through referrals and repeat work. Nothing else.

We turn up when we say we will. We quote fairly, in writing, before anything starts. We keep the site tidy, clean up the gutters, and leave the house drier than we found it. Nothing flashy. Nothing cut corners. Just proper workmanship from a firm your neighbours probably already use.

Whether it's a single slipped slate on a terrace, a leaking flat roof on a shop unit, or a full re-roof on a Victorian detached. You'll get the same response. A fair price, a clean job, a roof that holds up.

"If the rain's getting in, we'll fix it. If it isn't, we'll tell you straight." Tommy Smith · Owner, Home N Dry
