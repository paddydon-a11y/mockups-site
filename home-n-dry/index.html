what we do

One slipped slate, or a whole new roof.

Residential or commercial, tiny repair or a proper strip and re-roof. If it protects a building from the weather, chances are we fit it, fix it, or renew it.

Roof repairs & re-roofs

Slipped slates, missing tiles, leaks, valleys, flashings and full re-roofs on terraces, semis and detached.

Storm & weather damage

Emergency tarps, temporary covers and written insurance reports. Most storm jobs made watertight inside 24 hours.

EPDM rubber flat roofs

Seamless, single piece rubber roofs with a 20 year guarantee. Ideal for extensions, garages and dormers.

GRP fibreglass flat roofs

Strong fibreglass systems with a proper seamless finish, built for whatever the north west throws at it.

Torch-on felt roofing

Traditional multi layer felt with clean drip details and neat trims. A proven system at an honest price.

Fascias, soffits & gutters

Maintenance free UPVC fascias and soffits, new gutter runs, or just the annual clear out. We leave nothing behind.

Chimney repairs & repointing

Repointing, rebuilds, fresh flashings and cowls. Full stack rebuilds when they're had it, tidy repairs when they haven't.

Slate & tile roofing

Welsh slate, concrete, clay, or interlocking tiles. Matched in, repaired, or fully stripped and renewed.

Garages & outbuilding roofs

New roofs on garages, lean tos, sheds and summer houses. Flat, pitched, or whatever shape the thing is.

meet the gaffer

A Widnes firm built on word of mouth.

We've been looking after roofs around Widnes, Runcorn and Warrington for over two decades. What started as a one man outfit has grown, slowly and honestly, into a small trusted team. All through referrals and repeat work. Nothing else.

We turn up when we say we will. We quote fair, in writing, before anything starts. We keep the site tidy, clean up the gutters on the way out, and leave the house drier than we found it. Nothing flashy. No cut corners. Just proper work.

Whether it's one slipped slate on a terrace, a leaking shop roof, or a full re-roof on a Victorian detached: same response every time. Fair price. Clean job. Roof that holds up.
