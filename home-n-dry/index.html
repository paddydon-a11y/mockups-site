What We Do

One slate or a complete new roof — we cover the lot.

Roofing is our bread and butter, but the Home N Dry team handle all the jobs that keep a property watertight and looked after. Residential or commercial, large or small.

Roof Repairs & Replacements

Slipped slates, missing tiles, leaks or a full re-roof — we'll diagnose it honestly and quote in writing before anything starts.

Storm & Weather Damage

Emergency storm repairs, temporary covers and fast insurance reports. We'll get your property watertight the same day where possible.

EPDM Rubber Flat Roofs

Seamless, single-piece rubber flat roofs with a 20-year guarantee. Ideal for extensions, garages and dormers.

GRP Fibreglass Flat Roofs

High-strength fibreglass systems with a seamless finish, built to handle anything the weather in Widnes can throw at it.

Torch-On Felt Roofing

Traditional multi-layer felt roofs done properly, with neat trims and clean drip details. A tried-and-tested system at an honest price.

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

Maintenance-free UPVC fascias and soffits, new gutter runs, or blocked gutter clear-outs. We tidy up and take everything away.

Chimney Repairs & Repointing

Repointing, rebuilds, flashings and cowls. Full stack rebuilds when they've seen better days, fresh flashings when they haven't.

Slate & Tile Roofing

Welsh slate, concrete, clay or interlocking tiles — matched in, repaired or fully stripped and renewed to last decades.

Garages, Extensions & Outbuildings

New roofs on garages, lean-tos, sheds, summer houses and extensions — flat, pitched or anything in between.

General Building & Maintenance

Pointing, brickwork patches, rendering touch-ups, pathways and all the smaller jobs other trades leave behind.

About Home N Dry

A family-run Widnes firm built on word-of-mouth.

Home N Dry Property Maintenance has been looking after roofs and properties around Widnes, Runcorn, Warrington and wider Merseyside for over two decades. What started as a one-man roofing outfit has grown, honestly and slowly, into a small trusted team — entirely through referrals and repeat work.

Tommy and the lads turn up when they say they will, quote fairly in writing, keep the site tidy, and leave your home drier than they found it. Nothing flashy, nothing cut-corners — just proper workmanship from a firm your neighbours probably already use.

Whether it's a single slipped slate, a leaking flat roof on a commercial unit or a full re-roof on a Victorian terrace, you'll get the same response: a fair price, a clean job, and a roof that holds up.
