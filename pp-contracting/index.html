About PP Contracting

Local, hands-on and straight-talking.

Run by Paul Salmon and based in Retford, PP Contracting Limited covers domestic and commercial work within roughly 60 miles, from driveways and patios to full groundworks packages and general building.

It is a small, hands-on outfit, so the person who quotes the job is the person on site getting it done, and the lines of communication stay short from start to finish.
