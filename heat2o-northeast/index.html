What we do

A handful of trades, done properly.

We're not the outfit promising every job under the sun. Heat2O sticks to plumbing, heating and bathroom suites, work we know inside out. That's why our finishes hold up and our installs come in on schedule.

01
Bathroom suites
Full bathroom installs from the strip-out to the silicone bead. Walk-in showers, panelled baths, vanity units, tiling, underfloor heating; all wrapped up with a tidy site at the end of every day.
02
Plumbing
Domestic plumbing across the Northeast: leaks, repairs, copper and plastic pipework, mains pressure, taps, toilets and full re-plumbs. Twelve years of jobs done first time.
03
Heating & cylinders
Combi swaps, system boilers, unvented cylinders and full system flushes. Gas Safe registered with the certificates to match. Tidy install, properly commissioned, paperwork in your inbox.
04
Wet rooms & walk-ins
Tray-formed wet rooms and luxury walk-in showers, including waterproofing, tanking and large-format tile or stone-panel finishes. Built for the long run, not just the photo.
05
Designer radiators
Vertical anthracite, towel rails, traditional cast; sized and fitted to actually heat the room. We'll spec the right output for your space, not just whatever's in the showroom.
