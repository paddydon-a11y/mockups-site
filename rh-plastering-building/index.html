01 / What we do

A complete building & home improvement company

Plastering is where it started, and it's still what we're known for. Alongside it we take on the full build, from extensions and loft conversions to bathrooms, carpentry and the trades that finish a job off.

S/01 · Flagship

Plastering & general building

Skimming, full re-plastering and rendering with a clean, flat finish, backed by the general building work to put a room or a whole property right.

  • Skim, re-plaster & render
  • Smooth finish ready to decorate
  • Full building works alongside

Extensions

Single and double-storey rear and side extensions, from footings and brickwork through to plaster and finish.

Loft Conversions

Loft conversions and dormers creating new bedrooms, bathrooms and usable space in the roof.

Bathrooms

Full bathroom installs, including stripping out, plumbing, tiling and a clean finished room.

Carpentry

First and second-fix carpentry, roofing timbers, doors, skirting and fitted woodwork.

Bricklaying

Brick and blockwork for extensions, walls and structural alterations, built to last.

Plumbing & Electrics

First-fix plumbing and electrics carried out as part of the build, so the whole job is covered.

03 / The firm

Plastering roots, full building service

RH Plastering & Building is run by Ricky Harvey, a Norwich tradesman who started out plastering and grew into taking on the whole job, inside and out.

Because the building work, plastering, bathrooms, carpentry and trades are all handled by one team, you deal with one point of contact and one standard rather than chasing separate trades. Every job is priced clearly and left clean and finished.
