01 / What we do

The full drylining package, one team.

From steel framing to final skim, the whole lining and ceiling package sits with us. One team on site, so the programme isn't waiting on three different subbies and the finish lands ready for paint.
01
Metal Stud Partitions
Single, twin and staggered-stud partitions set out to drawing. Single and multi-board build-ups with deflection heads and openings framed ready for second fix.
02
MF & Feature Ceilings
MF, suspended, curved and circular feature ceilings set to laser line. Cove and shadow-gap detailing, service zones coordinated with M&E so the grid never fights the first fix above.
03
SFS / Steel Framing Systems
External infill walls, structural studs and framed bulkheads. Set out true and braced, with deflection heads and acoustic detailing built in from the start.
04
Bulkheads & Cove Details
Boxed bulkheads, drop details and LED cove troughs framed and boarded crisp. The fiddly geometry that makes a fit-out look designed, not just built.
05
Tape, Joint & Skim Finish
Level 3 to 5 finishes ready for paint or wallcovering. Three-coat jointing, taped angles and full skim coats, sanded and signed off for the trades behind us.
06
Wall Lining & Encasements
Dot & dab, independent linings, fire and acoustic build-ups, column casings and riser walls. Plumb, square and ready for the finish.

03 / How we work

Priced fair, run tidy, finished on time.

A team you actually know on site. We price the job, frame it, board it and finish it, so nothing gets lost between people.
Step 01

Price & programme

Send the drawings or get us on site. We come back quickly with a fair price and a clear idea of how the package sits against your programme.

Step 02

On the tools

Set out, framed, boarded and insulated to spec. We keep our area tidy, work to your sequence and flag anything that won't sit right before it's buried.

Step 03

Tape, skim & hand over

Joints taped, angles beaded, skim laid and sanded to the level you need. Walked off and handed over clean, ready for the trades coming behind us.
