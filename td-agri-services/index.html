What we do

Groundworks built for farms & land

From the first dig to the final level, TD Agri Services handles the muck, the stone and the concrete — so your yard, building or field works the way it should.

Agricultural Groundworks

Complete site preparation for farm buildings, livestock units, sheds and extensions — excavation, foundations, footings, drainage and hardstanding, dug and set out to take whatever the job demands.

Concrete Yards & Bases

Farm yards, livestock building bases, silage clamps, machinery pads and hardstanding — laid, levelled and built to last.

Farm Roads & Access Tracks

Stone and hardcore tracks, access roads, gateways and passing places that stand up to tractors, trailers and the weather.

Land Drainage & Ditching

Field drainage, ditching, culverts, soakaways and dyke clearance to keep land workable and water moving where it should.

Digger & Plant Hire

Excavators and dumpers supplied with an experienced operator, by the day or on contract — the right machine for the ground.

Site Clearance & Demolition

Clearing old buildings and overgrowth, demolition, muck away, grading and levelling — site left clean and ready to build.

Footings, Fencing & Earthworks

Footings for barns and extensions, stock and post-and-rail fencing, earthmoving, ponds and reclamation work.

About TD Agri Services

A local lad who knows the land

TD Agri Services is run by Tom Dainty, a groundworker based in Pilling, just outside Garstang. Brought up around farms and the Fylde, Tom takes on agricultural groundworks the way it should be done — dug right, drained right and finished properly, whatever the weather throws at it.

From a new concrete yard or building base to land drainage, access tracks and digger work, it's one local contractor who turns up, gets stuck in and leaves the job tidy.
