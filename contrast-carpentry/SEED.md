## Recipe
archetype: bespoke monochrome editorial (B-vibe, BRIGHT white) / hero:H2-split /
trust:T1 / services:S3-numbered-editorial / gallery:G2-asymmetric-masonry / about:after-gallery /
reviews:R3-2x2-local / contact:C1 / flourish: dovetail-joint-lock loader (NEW)
(Combo H2-split + S3-numbered + G2-masonry — NOT in banned-combos list.)

- **Surface:** BRIGHT — pure white `#ffffff` + cool stone cards `#f5f4f2`, near-black ink `#16130f`.
  The brand IS black-and-white "contrast", so lean monochrome: crisp ink on white, hairline rules.
  Bright, never warm cream.
- **Accent:** warm oak/timber tan `#a9763f` (deep `#84592b`) — pulled from the oak worktops in the
  work photos, references the trade, and adds the one warm note against the strict B&W. Primary
  buttons stay INK BLACK (max contrast, on-brand); oak used for labels, numerals, hairline accents.
  NOT in banned palette families (crisp-white+ink-black+warm-oak-tan).
- **Display type:** Montserrat (Light 300 wordmark / 600-700 headings) — matches the actual logo
  wordmark, wide letter-spacing. NOT in banned window. Body: Inter.
- **Signature:** the name "Contrast" → strict monochrome editorial, big serif-free numerals on a
  numbered service list, generous whitespace, hairline rules. Oak is the single warm accent.
- **Flourish (NEW loader concept):** "dovetail joint lock" — two oak-toned timber pieces with
  complementary dovetail (pins/tails) edges slide in from left and right and interlock into one
  solid board, a thin bar fills, then the CONTRAST CARPENTRY wordmark + "Kingswinford · West
  Midlands" subline settle. Joiner-specific; the catalogue listed joiner loader as "invent". Distinct
  from all banned trowel/roller/pointing/chisel/fence/storey-stack/portal-frame/hazard loaders.
