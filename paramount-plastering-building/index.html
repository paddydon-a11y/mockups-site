What we do

Seven trades.
One standard.

From a single skimmed ceiling to a full renovation, every job gets the same level of care: tidy work, honest advice and a finish that lasts.

Our specialty
01

Plastering

Flat, clean skimming over new boards or tired old walls, ready for paint. Ceilings, patch repairs and whole-house replastering, done properly.

02

Rendering

External rendering and internal render systems that protect the fabric of your home and sharpen up its appearance.

03

Porch Installation

Porches and canopies designed and built from footings to finish, adding space and kerb appeal at the front door.

04

Patios

Design, groundwork and precision laying. Porcelain and natural stone patios built on proper foundations, edged and finished to last.

05

Landscaping

Garden transformations that make outdoor space usable again: levels, drainage, borders, steps and planting areas.

06

Full Renovations

Whole-room and whole-house renovations managed start to finish, stripping back, rebuilding and finishing to a high standard.

07

Extensions

Single and double storey extensions that add real living space and value, built carefully and kept moving.
