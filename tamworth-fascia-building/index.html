Meet the team

Run by Chris Sale from Tamworth.

Tamworth Fascia & Building is the new name for Tamworth Fascia Force, a family-run business run by Chris Sale from his base in Tamworth. The rebrand reflects the way the work has grown: it is no longer just fascias, it is full building work from foundation to finish.

Most of our jobs are repeat clients or word-of-mouth referrals from across Tamworth, Lichfield and the surrounding villages. We are honest about timings, tidy on site, and we turn up when we say we will.
