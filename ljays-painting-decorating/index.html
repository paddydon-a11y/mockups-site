About Louise

I started this because I'm a bit obsessed with a tidy edge.

LJAY's is run by me, Louise Jones — a Falkirk-based painter and decorator with a soft spot for big colours and properly prepped walls. Every job starts the same way: a chat, a coffee, a proper look at the room and an honest quote. No surprises, no shortcuts, no half-finished promise to come back tomorrow.

I cover homes and small commercial spaces across Falkirk, Glasgow, Edinburgh and everywhere in between. Whether you've finally picked the colour or you've been stuck on swatches for six months, give me a shout.
