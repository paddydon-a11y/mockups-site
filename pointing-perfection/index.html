Specialists in pointing & brickwork

From precision repointing to full builds, restoration and property maintenance. You name it, we do it.

Who we are

An award-winning bricklayer who takes pride in every joint

Pointing Perfection is led by Matt, a fully qualified bricklayer with over 20 years on the tools, distinction from college and the St Abbott’s Cup to his name. Quality is the priority on every job, from a simple wall repair to a double-storey extension.
