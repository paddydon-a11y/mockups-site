The workshop

A small, careful team fitting honest fires. Everything surveyed, installed and signed off by the same hands, nothing sub-contracted.

We install stoves of every fuel — wood, multi-fuel, gas and electric. We replace tired flues, rebuild fireplaces from the brickwork out, and will happily fit a stove you've bought yourself. HETAS registered since the beginning.

Dean still surveys every job personally. If it isn't right on the day, it doesn't leave.
