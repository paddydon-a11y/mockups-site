Chapter II · The Principal

Run by Dean Sthake since the business lit its first stove in 2010.

Started as one man with a van doing honest flue work around Ware. Sixteen years later, Dean still surveys every job personally and still signs off every install before it leaves — the workshop is bigger, the trucks newer, the approach exactly the same.

HETAS registered, fully insured and trusted by families, pub landlords and developers from Stevenage to Hertford Heath. If a job isn't right, it doesn't get signed off.
