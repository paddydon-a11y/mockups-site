A Note From Desi

We've been building and finishing homes across Northern Ireland for the better part of two decades.

What started as one tradesman with a van has grown into a tight-knit team of joiners, fitters, plumbers and electricians, all under one roof. We don't subcontract the important bits and we don't move on until the snag list is empty.

These days most of the work is bespoke kitchens and bathrooms, with the joinery shop turning out fitted wardrobes, staircases, doors and the odd timber-framed extension. Every job, big or small, gets the same attention.

Desi McGibbon
Founder & Lead Joiner
