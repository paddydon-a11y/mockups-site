/ FAQ

Common questions.

If yours isn't here, just ring us, that's usually faster.

How fast can you get to me?+
Usually within 30 to 45 minutes for emergency call-outs. We'll give you an honest ETA when you ring, no padding to make ourselves look good.
Do you charge extra at night or on weekends?+
No. The rate is the rate. We quote before we set off and stick to it, whether it's 2pm Tuesday or 2am Sunday.
Will you damage my door to get me back in?+
Almost never. We pick or bypass on 99% of lockouts. Drilling is the absolute last resort and we'll always tell you before doing it.
Do you do lock upgrades after a break-in?+
Yes. Anti-snap, anti-bump, anti-pick cylinders, replacement multipoint mechanisms, sash jammers and door reinforcement. We can also help you talk to insurance.
What plumbing emergencies do you cover?+
Burst pipes, leak isolation, frozen pipes, stop tap failures, tank and cylinder leaks, toilet overflows, radiator splits, anything that needs water turning off and a permanent fix the same visit.
Do you have a landlord arrangement?+
Yes. We work with several local investors and landlords on a direct-call basis. Get in touch and we'll set up a single point of contact for your portfolio.
