About PWC

Knowledge, kit and reliability. Not an agency.

Piling & Welding Construction Ltd has been part of the piling industry for over 25 years. We bring extensive knowledge and the reliability to deliver, helping our clients hit their targets and their deadlines.

Every operative is qualified, coded and motivated: piling operators, crane operators, barge masters, site supervisors and coded welders, all carded for confined space, MEWP, working at height, SSSTS and SMSTS. We turn up ready to drive steel, not to make up the numbers.

  • Tubular, AZ, U, H-beam and timber piles, driven and welded to spec.
  • Leader rig, silent piler, side grip and conventional plant.
  • Multi-skilled team for marine, tidal and confined-space works.
