What we do

Painting, decorating and plasterboard. Done right.

From a single feature wall to a full house repaint, every job gets the same treatment: honest advice, careful prep and a finish you will want to show off.

01

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, woodwork, doors and staircases. Filled, sanded, caulked and painted with crisp cut lines and even coverage.

02

Exterior Painting

Render, masonry, fascias, windows and front doors. Weatherproof finishes that lift the whole front of the house.

03

Wallpapering & Feature Walls

Pattern matching, straight seams and clean trims. From a single statement wall to full rooms.

04

Plasterboard Walls & Ceilings

Stud partitions, boarding, ceilings and boxing-in. Taped, jointed and finished ready for decoration.

05

Repairs & Making Good

Cracks, holes, water stains and tired surfaces repaired and blended in before any paint goes on.

06

Full Room & House Refresh

Complete redecoration for lettings, sales or a fresh start. One trade, one point of contact, one tidy handover.
