The Craftsman

Richard Hall, master plasterer

RJ Hall Venetian Plastering is the work of Richard Hall, a master craftsman applying centuries-old Venetian and Roman plaster techniques to the South West's most exceptional homes.

Every finish is mixed, applied and burnished by hand, layer upon layer, until the surface holds the depth, movement and lustre that only true Venetian plaster can. No two walls are ever the same.
