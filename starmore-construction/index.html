About Starmore

Starmore Construction is a Manchester-based construction company delivering bespoke joinery, certified fire door installations, fire stopping and full multi-trade project management across the North West.

As a certified BMTrada door installer and fire stopping specialist, we bring a level of compliance and quality assurance that sets us apart.
