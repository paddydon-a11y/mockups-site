What We Do

Electrical Services

From consumer unit upgrades and full rewires to EV charger installations and media walls — MM Spark delivers meticulous electrical work across Wigan and beyond.

EV Charger Installation

Professional electric vehicle charger installation for your home or business. Fully certified with all necessary electrical upgrades included.

Consumer Unit Upgrades

Fuse board and consumer unit replacements to current BS 7671 regulations. RCD protection, surge protection devices, and full testing and certification.

Full & Partial Rewires

Complete house rewires bringing older properties up to current safety standards. Minimal disruption, professionally finished and fully certified.

Lighting Systems

Interior and exterior lighting design and installation. LED upgrades, spotlights, downlights, garden and security lighting — energy-efficient solutions.

Media Wall Electrics

Custom socket and cabling solutions for media walls. Hidden wiring, recessed sockets, and LED backlighting for a clean, professional finish.

Kitchen Electrical

Complete kitchen electrical installations including cooker circuits, socket upgrades, under-cabinet lighting, and appliance connections for new kitchen fit-outs.

Electrical Testing & EICR

Electrical inspection condition reports (EICR) for landlords, homeowners, and commercial premises. PAT testing and fixed wire testing available.

Sockets & Switches

Additional sockets, USB charging points, smart switches, and dimmer installations. Upgrading your home's electrics for modern living.
