What We Do

Skilled brickwork, clean plastering, serious building work

Two trades under one roof. Whether it's a single garden wall, a full single-storey extension, or the plastering job that finishes it off — we handle it properly, start to finish.

All Aspects of Brickwork

From facing brick to decorative courses, chimney rebuilds to structural blockwork. Clean lines, tight joints, level every time.

Extensions

Single & double-storey extensions. Full build from foundations to finish — brickwork, steels, plastering, every trade coordinated.

New Builds

Working on new-build projects alongside main contractors. Trusted by developers for consistent quality and reliable timelines.

Garden Walls

Straight, curved, piered or decorative. Built on proper foundations to last decades, not seasons. Plenty of reference jobs locally.

Stone Patios

Indian sandstone, porcelain, block paving. Laid on a proper base with correct falls for drainage — not just slapped on sand.

Knock-Throughs & Steels

RSJ installation, load-bearing wall removal, opening-up works. All done with proper structural engineer drawings and building control sign-off.

Internal Plastering

Skim coats, full re-skims, board & skim. A mirror-flat finish ready to paint — no snagging, no cracks, no waiting weeks for it to dry.

Patch Repairs

Wall cracks, damaged plaster, brick pointing gone bad, small patches from electrical chasing — tidied up and made invisible.

Stud Walls & Plasterboarding

Timber or metal stud partitions built to spec. Plasterboarded, taped and skimmed ready for decorators.

Chimney Rebuilds

Full chimney rebuilds, re-pointing, flashing and lead work. Scaffold access handled — we organise what's needed.

About SBP

Scott Patterson, on the job — not behind a desk.

SBP is built around one simple idea: do a proper job and treat the customer like a neighbour, not a number. Scott has been laying bricks and skimming walls since he was an apprentice, and that hands-on experience is what shows up on every job.

We work across Bishops Stortford, Harlow, Sawbridgeworth, Epping, Saffron Walden, Great Chesterford, Buntingford, Ware and Hertford — from a single afternoon's patch repair up to full extensions. Whatever the size, the standard's the same.
