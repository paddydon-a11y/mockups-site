What I do

Decorating, inside and out

From a single feature wall to a whole house, plus exterior painting and fencing — one trusted pair of hands for the lot.

Interior Painting

Walls, ceilings, skirting and trim — cut in clean and rolled smooth, on new builds and lived-in homes alike.

Wallpapering

Patterned papers and statement walls hung dead straight and pattern-matched, no creases or lifted seams.

Murals & Feature Walls

Wall murals, metallic papers and bold feature walls — the kind of finish that makes a room.

Panelling & Woodwork

Wall panelling, wainscoting, doors, spindles and banisters — hand-finished in matt, satin or gloss.

Staircases & Period Work

Stripping, filling and restoring tired staircases and older homes back to a crisp, fresh finish.

Exterior & Fencing

Outside painting plus fencing, gates and general external work to smarten up the outside of your home.
