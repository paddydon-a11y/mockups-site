What We Do

Bricks first.
Everything else covered.

Specialists in brickwork and groundwork, set up to manage the full job. If a project needs joinery, roofing or interior finishes, we plug in the right trades and stay on the tools.

01 / Specialty

Brickwork

Garden walls, retaining walls, brick piers, feature curves and brick repairs. Matched bricks and mortar where we can find them, and a clean string-line throughout.

02 / Specialty

Groundwork

Footings, drainage, oversite slabs, mini-digger excavation, base preparation for patios, sheds and extensions. Set up for clean handover to the next trade.

03 / Service

Extensions

Single-storey extensions, side returns, kitchen builds. Brick and block to building-reg specs, with everyone we trust pulled in to finish to your spec.

04 / Service

Patios & Paving

Porcelain, sandstone, block paving and resin laid on properly prepared bases. Falls calculated, drainage in, and joints kept tight.

05 / Service

Renovations

Whole-property refurbs, knock-throughs, structural openings and internal rebuilds. We coordinate the trades so you only have one number to call.

06 / Service

Outdoor Builds

Pizza ovens, BBQ stations, fire pits, pergolas and bespoke outdoor kitchens. The good stuff, built to last through North East weather.
