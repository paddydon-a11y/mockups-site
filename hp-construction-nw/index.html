About Us

Bury born, Bury based. Trusted by homeowners across Greater Manchester.

HP Construction (NW) was founded by Jonathan Purves on a simple idea: give people in Bury a builder they can actually rely on. One number, one point of contact, one team that turns up, finishes the job and leaves the place cleaner than we found it.

We handle the full package, from planning drawings and Building Regs through groundworks, brickwork, structural, first fix, joinery and decorating. Whether it is a single storey rear extension, a dormer loft or a bespoke piece of fitted joinery, the standard is the same.

How We Work

Four steps. No surprises.

Here is exactly what happens once you pick up the phone. Same process every time, whether it is a new staircase or a full two-storey extension.

01

Site Visit

Jonathan comes out to your property within a few days, walks the job with you and answers every question. No pressure, no hard sell.

02

Written Quote

You get a proper itemised quote within 48 hours. Fixed price where possible. Every line explained so you know what you are paying for.

03

On Site

Start date agreed, crew turns up on time. Daily sweep, dust sheets down, weekly catch-ups so you are never left wondering what is happening.

04

Snagging & Sign-Off

We do our own snagging before handover. Certificates, Building Regs sign-off and a 10 year workmanship guarantee in writing.
