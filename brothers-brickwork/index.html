What we do

Brick & build, done properly.

From a single garden wall to a full extension, we cover the brick and building trades, all from one reliable local team.

Brickwork & Blockwork

Facing brick, blockwork and structural walls, built to true lines with clean, consistent coursing and pointing.

Garden & Boundary Walls

Front and garden walls, piers, capping and brick-on-edge, finished ready for railings, gates or fencing.

Retaining Walls

Engineered retaining walls for sloping gardens and split levels, built with proper footings, fill and drainage.

Extensions & Structural

Single and double-storey extension brickwork, openings, steel padstones and structural alterations.

Repointing & Brick Repairs

Raking out and repointing, crack stitching, rebuilds and matching brick and mortar on older properties.

Patios, Paving & Landscaping

Sandstone and porcelain patios, paths, steps and full garden makeovers laid level and built to last.

Foundations & Groundworks

Footings, trenches, oversite, drainage and the groundworks that get a job started off solid.

Brick Features & Restoration

Brick BBQs, planters, pillars and steps, plus general building and restoration to bring period brickwork back to life.

Who we are

A family-run brickwork team you can rely on.

Brothers Brickwork is a family-run firm of time-served bricklayers and builders working across East London. We take on everything from garden and boundary walls to retaining walls, extensions, repointing and full garden makeovers.

We turn up when we say, keep the site tidy and build it the way it should be built, with neat coursing, clean pointing and proper footings. Most of our work comes from homeowners who've seen a wall or a job we've done nearby.

We're bricklayers and builders, so we stick to what we do best. We don't take on gas or electrical work, but we'll happily point you towards a trusted trade if you need one.
