What we do

Every roofing job, done right.

From complete new roofs to repairs, leadwork and chimneys, Shoreside Roofing covers it all across Hampshire, Wiltshire and Dorset, with a tidy site and a watertight finish.

01

New Roofs

Complete new roofs in tile or slate, built on the right battens, membrane and detailing to last for decades.

02

Re-Roofs

Stripping tired, leaking roofs back and re-covering them properly, upgrading felt, battens and ventilation as we go.

03

Roof Repairs

Slipped tiles, leaks, storm damage and flat-roof failures sorted fast, so a small problem stays a small problem.

04

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

Crisp new uPVC fascias, soffits and gutters that protect the roof edge and smarten up the whole house.

05

Chimney Capping & Pointing

Re-pointing, re-capping, cowls and full chimney repairs to stop damp and keep the stack sound.

06

Lead Works

Expert leadwork on valleys, flashings and abutments, dressed and sealed to keep water out where roofs meet walls.

We also do: Moss scraping Velux window installation Roof inspections
