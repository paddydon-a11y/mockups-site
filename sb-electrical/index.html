S&B Electrical Services is run by Conor — a qualified, NAPIT-registered electrician covering Walsall, Rugeley and the wider West Midlands. Domestic, commercial and industrial — all handled with the same attention to detail and the same commitment to doing things right.

We're not the kind of sparks who rush a job, leave a mess, and move on. Every cable is clipped properly, every board is labelled clearly, every test is documented. The work you see in our photos? That's the standard. Every time.

Whether it's a full rewire, an EV charger, a lighting scheme that makes your kitchen look like it belongs in a magazine, or a commercial fit-out on a deadline — the answer is the same: we'll do it properly, we'll certify it, and we'll leave it tidy.
