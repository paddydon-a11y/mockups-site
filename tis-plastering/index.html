About TIS Plastering

A plasterer who answers the phone, shows up, and leaves the place cleaner than he found it.

Tedi Stancioiu has been plastering across the Midlands since 2013. What started as domestic work — small rooms, full house replasters, awkward stairwells — has grown into a reputation that now has major house builders and developers on the repeat-book list.

The job doesn't change depending on who's paying. Whether it's a single ceiling overboard for a homeowner or a full estate of new builds for a contractor, the finish is the same: flat, polished, and ready for paint. No mess left behind. No disappearing act halfway through.

What we do

Every type of plaster job, finished properly.

From a single wall skim to a full development contract — if it needs plastering, we do it.

01

Skimming & Plaster Finishing

Smooth, flat skim over existing walls and ceilings — ready for paint, wallpaper or tile. The bread and butter job, done to a painter-ready standard every time.

02

Hardwall Plastering

Two-coat hardwall and multi-finish for masonry walls. Strong base for tiling, cladding or skim. Ideal for bathrooms, extensions and new build shells.

03

Dot & Dab Plasterboarding

Plasterboard bonded directly to solid walls with adhesive. Fast, clean, and perfect for refurbishments where you need a flat surface without losing much room.

04

Ceiling Overboarding & Skim

Artex, cracked or damaged ceilings over-boarded and skimmed to a factory-flat finish. Faster and cleaner than a full ceiling removal.

05

Rendering & External Finishes

Sand and cement render and finish coats on external walls, extensions and new builds. Weatherproof, painter-ready and built to last.

06

Plasterboard & Stud Walls

Full stud wall builds, insulation and boarding. Room dividers, loft partitions, commercial fit-out — boarded and taped to a skim-ready level.

07

Damp Patch Repair & Re-Skim

Patch repairs for damp, knock-through work, removed chimney breasts or electrician's chases. Blended back in so you can't tell where the job was.

08

New Build & Developer Contracts

Volume plastering for house builders and main contractors across the Midlands. On-programme, priced per plot, and with the insurance and paperwork site managers expect.
