About

Straight walls, straight answers.

Droitbuilding Ltd is run by Graham Bale. Droit is French for straight, and that is the standard the firm is named after: straight brickwork, straight pricing, straight talk when something needs deciding. Graham prices every job himself and keeps the same team on site from dig to decoration.
