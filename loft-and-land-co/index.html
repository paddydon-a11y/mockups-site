What We Offer

Our services

Everything you need to make the most of your loft space.

Loft Boarding

Professional boarding using high-quality chipboard or loft panels. We raise the boards above your insulation so nothing is compressed, keeping your home energy-efficient.

Loft Insulation

Top-up or replace your existing insulation to meet current building regulations. Reduce heat loss, lower energy bills and keep your home warmer in winter.

Loft Hatches & Ladders

New or upgraded loft hatches with folding or sliding loft ladders for safe, easy access. No more balancing on chairs or wobbly stepladders.

Loft Lighting

LED lighting installed so you can see exactly what’s in your loft. Battery-powered or mains-wired options available.

Storage Solutions

Complete loft storage packages including boarding, ladder, hatch, lighting and insulation. Everything sorted in a single visit.

Landscaping & Groundwork

It’s not just lofts! We also offer landscaping, fencing, patios and garden maintenance across Staffordshire.
