04 · Process

Quoted Monday.
Up by Friday.

01

Site visit

Dan or one of the lads comes out, walks the property, takes a tape and a few pictures. No-obligation quote inside 48 hours.

02

Booked in

You confirm. We pencil a date and email across the method statement, risk assessment and insurance certs so your trade has them ready.

03

Up & tagged

Erected on the agreed morning. Inspected, tagged, signed off and handed over. Ready for your roofer or builder to start the same day.

04

Struck & clean

Job done, give us a ring, we come back, strike the lot and sweep up. Final invoice goes out after we're off site, never before.

Tubes & fittings

Galvanised tube, double couplers, sleeve couplers, swivels and right-angles. Inspected and tagged every job.

Boards & toe-boards

Grade A scaffold boards, toe-boards and brick guards across every lift. Swapped the moment they show wear.

System scaffold

Modular system kit on site for fast, repeatable rigs on commercial jobs, new builds and complex shapes.

Paperwork tied off

Method statements, risk assessments, scaff tags, hand-over sheets and insurance schedule on request.
