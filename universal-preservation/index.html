What We Do

Plastering and rendering, day in, day out. With a specialism that pays the deposit on the next job.

Most weeks we’re skimming, rendering or finishing extensions like any decent plastering outfit. The difference is that for forty-seven years we’ve also been the firm South London calls when there’s damp, woodworm or dry rot underneath the walls that need plastering.

01 / Core trade

Plastering & rendering

The bread and butter. Skim coats, hardwall backings, sand & cement render, monocouche, K-Rend, pebbledash and heritage lime work. Forty-seven years of trowel time means walls go on flat the first time.

  • Skim & hardwall plastering
  • Sand & cement rendering
  • Monocouche & K-Rend systems
  • Pebbledash & specialist finishes
  • Heritage lime plastering
02 / Specialism

Damp proof course

Rising damp, penetrating damp, salt-contaminated walls. We hack out the failed plaster, inject chemical or membrane DPC, then re-plaster on a salt-resistant finish that won’t blow back through in eighteen months. 30-year written guarantee.

  • Chemical DPC injection
  • Membrane & tanking systems
  • Salt-resistant re-plastering
  • Cellar & basement waterproofing
  • 30-year written guarantee
03 / Specialism

Woodworm & dry rot

Active infestation or a job that’s been left ten years too long — we’ll survey honestly. Timber spray treatment, dry rot eradication, joist replacement, masonry treatment. Then re-plaster the lot once everything’s sound.

  • Wood-boring beetle treatment
  • Dry rot & wet rot eradication
  • Joist & lintel replacement
  • Floor void ventilation
  • Pre-purchase timber surveys

How We Work

Survey first. Quote second. Properly diagnose before we touch a wall.

Whether it’s a straight skim or a damp course, we look behind the obvious before we quote. It’s why our work lasts and why most of our jobs come from word of mouth.

i

Survey

Free in-person inspection. Moisture meter readings, salt-band checks, timber probes — we look behind the obvious before we call it.

ii

Diagnose

Plain English report on what’s wrong, what’s causing it, and the smallest amount of work that’ll properly fix it. No upsell.

iii

Treat

Hack out, treat, re-build. Damp proof course, timber treatment or fresh render — clean site, dust sheets, proper finishing.

iv

Guarantee

Written 30-year guarantee on damp proof course work, transferable if you sell. We come back if anything moves.
