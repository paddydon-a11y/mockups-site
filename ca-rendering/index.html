What we do

Rendering & coating, done properly

Silicone Render

Through-coloured silicone render that sheds water, breathes and stays clean, a low-maintenance finish in the colour you choose. No painting needed.

Through-Coloured

Monocouche Render

One-coat through-coloured render applied by hand or machine, scraped back to a clean, even texture, a tough, modern finish for new builds and renovations.

One-Coat System

Textured Wall Coatings

Weatherproof, flexible masonry coatings that seal and protect tired walls, available in a wide range of colours to bring a property back to life.

Masonry Coatings

External Wall Insulation

Insulated render systems that warm the home and transform the look in one go, boards, mesh and render carrier built up to a smart, finished face.

EWI Systems

Render Repairs

Cracked, blown or patchy render cut out, made good and blended in, so repairs disappear and the wall is sound and watertight again.

Crack & Patch

Pebbledash & Re-Render

Old pebbledash and failing render stripped off and replaced with a clean modern finish, a fresh look that protects the wall underneath.

Strip & Renew

Who you're dealing with

C&A Rendering & Wall Coating

C&A Rendering and Wall Coating is run by Craig Hields, a Newcastle and Gateshead renderer doing hands-on work, set up, mixed and applied properly, then scraped, floated and finished clean.

The work is exterior rendering and protective wall coatings: silicone render, monocouche, textured weatherproof coatings, repairs and pebbledash removal. The aim is simple, a wall that looks sharp and keeps the North East weather where it belongs, with no mess left behind.
