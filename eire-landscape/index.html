// what we do

Six things,
done properly.

I keep the menu small on purpose. Six jobs I've been doing long enough to do in my sleep, with the same suppliers, the same pricing, the same finish every time. no jack-of-all-trades carry on.

most popular

Patios

Indian sandstone, slate, porcelain, riven flag. Dug out properly, sub-base on grade, joints pointed by hand.

Decking

Hardwood, composite, raised platforms, integrated steps. Pressure-treated subframe, screwed not nailed.

Garden Rooms

Timber-frame summerhouses and home offices. Insulated, wired, finished how a real room should be.

Lawns & Turfing

Fresh roll-out turf, levelled topsoil, edges straight as a die. Watering schedule written on the back of the invoice.

Fencing

Closeboard, slatted, lap, gravel boards, posts in concrete. Whole runs replaced or matched in panel by panel.

Driveways

Block paving, resin-bound, gravel with edging. Drained off the road, not into your neighbour's garden.
