Meet the firm

Run by Jez. On every job.

HTI Decorating Services started out three decades ago with one van and a phone number. Today it's still run the same way, just with more colours on the chart and a bit more grey in the hair.

We work all over Swansea and out into the valleys. Most of our work comes from word of mouth, repeat customers, or someone who's seen the van round the corner. That's the way we like it. If the work's right, the next job tends to land on its own.
