How a job runs

Quote on Monday.
Up by Friday.

Most domestic jobs run on this rough timeline. We'll tell you straight if yours is going to take longer.

STEP 01

You ring or email

Send a photo if you can. We'll have a fair idea of price and lead time before we even drive over.

STEP 02

Site visit, fixed quote

We come out, take measurements, talk through the work. Written quote within 48 hours.

STEP 03

Scaffold & start

Scaffold goes up first. The roofing team follows. We protect the gardens and clean up at the end of every day.

STEP 04

Sign-off & strike

Walk-round with you, sign off the job, then strike scaffold. Final invoice goes after you're happy.

About Advanced Roofing & Scaffolding

Roof and scaffold under one roof.

Advanced Roofing & Scaffolding is the sister firm to Advanced Builders, run by John Wood from the same yard in Stamford. Most contractors hire scaffold in for roofing jobs, which means waiting on someone else's diary. We don't. Both halves run from one office.

That means a roof job moves at our pace, not a sub-contractor's. It also means if you just need scaffold up for a decorator, painter or another trade, we can do that on its own.
