About AFS

The detail that most people miss is the detail we were built to get right.

Accurate Fixing Solutions is a specialist facade contractor based in Fitzrovia, London. We deliver full installation services across glazing, cladding, curtain walling and associated facade systems — from design coordination right through to handover and aftercare.

Our portfolio includes landmark developments across London and the UK. Every one of them demanded precision, programme discipline and an obsessive attention to the things that matter — the joints, the tolerances, the finishes that turn a building envelope into architecture.

Capabilities

What we deliver.

01

Facade Installation

Windows, doors, glazing packages, stick-built and unitised curtain walling — complete facade systems delivered and installed, with every component coordinated and accounted for.

02

Recladding

Building safety, regulatory compliance, or simply a building that deserves better. We strip back and reclad with systems that perform and facades that transform.

03

Facade Maintenance & Repair

Routine cleaning, scheduled inspections, component repairs and replacements. We keep facades performing long after the scaffolding comes down.

04

Safety & Compliance

Every regulation met. Every protocol followed. We don't treat safety as a checkbox — it's built into how we plan, how we work and how we hand over.

05

Project Management

Programme scheduling, cost control, subcontractor coordination and quality assurance. One team overseeing everything so nothing gets lost between trades.

06

Full System Integration

Every bracket, seal, gasket and panel working together. We integrate full facade systems so the building envelope performs as a single, unified assembly.
