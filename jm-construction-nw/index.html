About JM Construction

Quality craftsmanship, built on trust

JM Construction NW Ltd is a trusted building company based in Chorley, run by Joe, delivering high-quality work across Lancashire. We cover extensions, renovations, kitchens, conversions and full design & build projects.

From the first drawing to the final detail, you deal with Joe throughout, with the same care, communication and craftsmanship on every job, whether it’s a single room or a whole-house transformation.

We manage the full process in-house, from design and groundwork through to the finished, decorated space, leaving every home better than we found it.

“We build it as if it were our own.”

What we do

From heritage restorations to contemporary new builds, every project is completed to the highest standard.

  • 01

    Extensions

    Single and double-storey extensions that add real space and value, built and finished to the highest standard inside and out.

  • 02

    Renovations & Refurbishments

    Full property renovations, from reconfiguring layouts and removing walls through to a complete fit-out, flooring and decorative finish.

  • 03

    Kitchens & Bathrooms

    Bespoke kitchens, bathrooms and wet rooms, designed, fitted, tiled and finished throughout by one trusted team.

  • 04

    Loft & Garage Conversions

    Turning unused lofts and garages into beautiful, practical living space that adds bedrooms, bathrooms and value to your home.

  • 05

    Design & Build

    A complete service from initial design and drawings through to construction and finishing, all managed in-house by Joe and the team.

  • 06

    Joinery & Staircases

    Bespoke joinery, staircases, panelling and fitted storage, crafted and installed with the meticulous attention to detail our clients rave about.
