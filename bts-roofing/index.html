What We Do

From flat roofing and single ply to lead work, slating, tiling, fascias, soffits and guttering — all aspects of roofing covered.

Flat Roofing

Torch-on felt (Langley approved) and EPDM Single Ply installations for domestic extensions, garages and large commercial roofs. 15–20 year manufacturer guarantees available.

Slating & Tiling

Complete strip-and-retile with new battens and breathable membranes. All types of slate and tile — fitted to last decades with quality materials throughout.

Single Ply & Resitrix

Lead Work

Expert lead welding — flashings, dormers, box gutters and complete lead bay roofs. Fully insured for all hot work with precision craftsmanship.

Commercial Roofing

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

Full replacement of uPVC fascia boards, soffits and guttering. Remove rotten timber and fit maintenance-free roofline products built to last.

Precision Work, Valued Service

With over 20 years’ experience in all aspects of roofing, BTS Roofing Contractors prides itself on precision work and valued customer service. Our policy is a simple one: the client is priority, safety is paramount and quality of work is imperative.

We specialise in a variety of flat roofing installations including small domestic extensions and garages to large commercial roofs, from torch-on felt (Langley approved) to EPDM Single Ply. We also undertake lead welding projects from flashings and small dormers to box gutters and complete lead bay roofs.

As an approved Resitrix Single Ply installer, we offer a 20-year manufacturer’s guarantee and can also provide a 15-year manufacturer’s guarantee with all our felt installations. We are fully insured for all hot work.
