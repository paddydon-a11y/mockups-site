The Studio

Sixteen years between the first sketch and the signed-off plans.

A quiet studio. Drawings done at a desk, sketches done on graph paper, every joint thought about before it gets built.

S
Steven Lee Bunney Director · Architectural Technologist · CIAT

Visualise it Studios is the practice of Steven Lee Bunney, an architectural technologist trained in Cardiff and working across South Wales and the West since 2010. The work covers domestic architecture in all its forms: new builds and bespoke houses, extensions and loft conversions, retrofits and Passive Haus design, with a soft spot for older housing stock that wants to live another hundred years.

Stages run concept to construction. Hand sketches on the kitchen table, Revit models on the screen, a 1:20 detail when the builder needs to know exactly what to do.
