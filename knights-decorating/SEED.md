## Direction
Heritage-confident, not warm-paper editorial and not childish-playful. The logo is a navy
heraldic knight crest with a serif wordmark; the real work is period/county (stone barn with
sage Farrow-&-Ball-toned doors, oak doors, designer radiator). Lean into that: clean BRIGHT
surface (white + cool stone), deep heraldic navy as the brand ink, heritage **sage green** as
the accent (pulled straight from the barn doors). Definitely NOT cream/eggshell.

wordmark fades in. New concept — deliberately NOT the roller-sweep (saturated 3x for decorators:
dexx, mj-mac, nwf). Not in banned-loader list.
