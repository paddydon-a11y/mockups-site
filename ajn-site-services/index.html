What we make

Steel and glass, fabricated to fit your space.

Bespoke Staircases
Floating oak treads, steel stringers and glass sides, made to measure and built into your space as the centrepiece of the room.
Glass & Stainless Steel Balustrades
Polished stainless posts with toughened glass infill, for stairs, landings and mezzanines.
Frameless Glass Balustrades
Channel-fixed glass with no posts. Decking, terraces and rooftops.
Metal Gates & Railings
Galvanised and powder-coated for driveways, gardens and boundaries.
Glass Canopies
Bolt-fixed structural glass over entrances and walkways.
Glass Doors & Partitions
Crittall-style steel screens and frameless internal glazing that open up a room without losing the light.
Aluminium Internal Doors
Origin slimline aluminium door sets for light, flow and a clean finish.

About AJN

The same hands, start to finish.

AJN Site Services has been fabricating and installing architectural metalwork and glass across Chesterfield and Derbyshire for over 25 years. From a single floating staircase to a full commercial fit-out, every job is measured, made and fitted by us.

We work directly with homeowners, builders, architects and commercial clients, handling the design, the steel, the glass and the install as one job.

  • Free site survey and quotation
  • Domestic and commercial projects
  • Bespoke fabrication to your drawings or ours
