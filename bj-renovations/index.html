03 / About us

More than a building company

B&J Renovations is your trusted renovation partner. We've built a solid reputation across London for quality, honesty and reliability, with decades of combined experience in construction and design, working on homes, shops, restaurants and commercial properties.

Whether it's a kitchen refit in North London or a full house refurbishment in the South, we take pride in delivering work that stands the test of time. Licensed plumbers, certified electricians and Gas Safe engineers are all part of the team, so the whole project is handled under one roof, and we're fully insured with public liability cover up to £5 million.

We listen, plan and deliver exactly what you need: only the finest materials and workmanship, no shortcuts and no hidden costs, completed on time and on budget.
