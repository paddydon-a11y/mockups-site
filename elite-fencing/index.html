// 02 What We Build

Eight things we do properly,
nothing we don't.

Every Elite job comes with the same fixed quote, the same materials brief, the same ten-year guarantee. No surprises after the deposit.

EFS / 01
Closeboard Fencing

Pressure-treated featherboard panels on concrete posts and gravel boards. Capped, finished, and built to take a Welsh winter.

EFS / 02
Slatted Contemporary

Horizontal slats with even gaps. Modern look, lets light through, perfect for tighter modern gardens.

EFS / 03
Picket Fencing

Classic picket fencing for front gardens and boundaries. Painted or stained, hung on concrete spurs, built to last.

EFS / 04
Bespoke Gates

Side gates, double driveway gates, garden gates. Hardwood or aluminium, hung on stainless hinges with proper closers.

EFS / 05
Decking

Composite or timber, on heavy joists and deep foundations. Glass or slatted balustrades to finish.

EFS / 06
Patios + Paving

Indian sandstone, porcelain, granite. Cut, bedded on full mortar, slurry-pointed. The proper way.

EFS / 07
Sleeper Walls

Pressure-treated oak or pine sleepers tied with rebar. Retaining walls, raised beds, stepped terraces.

EFS / 08
Full Landscaping

Fence, deck, patio, lawn, lighting. One specialist outfit, one phone number, one fixed price.
