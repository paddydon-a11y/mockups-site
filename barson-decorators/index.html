What we do

Seven services. One careful hand.

From a single feature wall to a full house repaint, every job gets the same approach — proper prep, quality materials, and a finish we'd be happy with at home.

— 01

Interior painting & woodwork.

Walls, ceilings, skirtings, architraves, doors. Hand-cut lines, proper dust-sheets, no roller spray on the laminate.

— 02

Exterior painting & rendering.

Doors, fascias, render, cladding and garage doors. Weather-grade paints, done in the window of dry days you actually get in England.

— 03

Wallpaper hanging.

Patterned, panelled, feature walls & full rooms — pattern match, no seams.

— 04

Kitchen & furniture respraying.

New-kitchen finish at a fraction of the cost. Tough, factory-grade lacquer.

— 05

Feature walls & colour.

Half-walls, panelled dados, bold colour blocks. We'll help you pick.

— 06

Kids rooms & murals.

Character walls, themes, ceiling skies. The fun stuff.

Specialism

Kitchen respraying.

A full kitchen repaint in two to three days, with a durable factory-grade lacquer finish. Save 60–80% versus a full replacement and keep your cupboards out of landfill.
