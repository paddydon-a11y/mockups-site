The mason

Nicolae Radu

Nicolae Radu Stonemasonry is hands-on work, stone graded, dressed and laid by Nicolae himself, from setting out the first course to striking the last joint clean.

The work is traditional natural stone: dry stone walling, garden and boundary walls, patios and repointing, with the patience old stone asks for. No shortcuts, no mess left behind, just a wall that does its job for the long run.
