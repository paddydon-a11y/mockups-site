04 · How it goes

Quoted. Surveyed.
Fitted in a day.

01

Free survey

Gordon comes round, takes accurate measurements, talks through colours, glass, hardware. Tell him what you want it to look like.

02

Fixed quote

Written quote within the week. Fixed price, line-by-line. Includes door, frame, fittings, fitting and any made-good plaster or render.

Door arrives

Manufacturer turnaround on the door is usually 2-3 weeks. We confirm your fit-date the day the door lands in our workshop.

About ThermaView Scotland

Run by Gordon. Fitted by Gordon.

ThermaView Scotland is a small, owner-run firm that has spent two decades fitting doors and windows in some of the country's most weather-beaten properties. Granite tenements in Aberdeen, harled cottages in the glens, sandstone semis along the Fife coast.

You speak to Gordon when you call. Gordon comes to do the survey. Gordon and the same fitting team turn up to do the work. No call centres, no sub-contractors handed a job sheet, no surprise faces. The person who quoted is the person who fits.
