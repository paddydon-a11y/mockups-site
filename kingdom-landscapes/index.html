Patios & Porcelain Paving

About Kingdom Landscapes

Local, experienced and genuinely proud of the work.

Kingdom Landscapes is a Brighton-based landscaping company run by Shaun Head, with an experienced team of around 10 covering every size of project. From porcelain patios and decking to garden walls, block paving and full landscape designs, we cover every part of the build in-house.

We're a Checkatrade-approved firm, and most of our work comes from recommendations and repeat customers across Sussex, which tells you everything about how we like to leave a garden.

  • Every job designed and built by our own team, never sub-contractors
  • Quality materials and proper preparation on every base and fall
  • Clean, tidy sites and clear communication start to finish
