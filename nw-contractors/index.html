What we do

Groundwork, start to finish

01

Site Clearance & Muck Away

Strip outs, dig downs, levelling and muck shifted off site so the build can start on clean ground.

02

Foundations & Footings

Trench fill, strip footings and reinforced slabs dug, shuttered and poured to building control spec.

03

Drainage & Sewer Connections

Foul and surface water drainage, soakaways and mains connections laid, bedded and tested.

04

Driveways & Access Roads

Tarmac, block paving and stone driveways, paths and access tracks on a properly compacted sub-base.

05

Concrete & Hardstanding

Garage bases, shed bases, yards and hardstanding poured level and finished to take the load.

06

Land & Agricultural Works

Field drainage, ground shaping, farm tracks and yard concreting for farms and smallholdings.

About N.W. Contractors

South Wales ground, safe hands

N.W. Contractors is a South Wales groundwork outfit run by Nigel Walters. From the first dig down to the last drain test, we handle the work below ground that everything above it depends on.

We work for builders, developers, farms and homeowners across South Wales, turning up when we say we will and leaving the site tidy when we leave. One call, and the groundwork is off your plate.
