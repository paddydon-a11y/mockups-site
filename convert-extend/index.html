About

Traditional craft, modern methods.

Convert Extend is a design and build company run by Steve, with a head office in Ealing and a second base in Buckinghamshire. Lorraine runs the office, so there is always someone on the end of the phone.

Most of the work is on Victorian houses: lofts, rear extensions and full renovations. Design, planning and build under one contract means one team and one point of contact from the first drawing to handover.
