About ZOLLY

A Gloucester decorator built on referrals.

ZOLLY Painting & Decorating is run by Zoltan Barbely — a painter, decorator and plasterer who's grown almost entirely through word of mouth and happy customers recommending him to friends and neighbours.

That only happens one way: turning up when you say you will, treating people's homes with respect, and leaving a finish you'd be proud to show off. Whether it's one room or the whole place, you'll deal with Zoltan from quote to clean-up.
