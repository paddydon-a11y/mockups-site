01 / What we do

Two trades, one trusted finish

SP Plastering & Rendering Services is a Wolverhampton plasterer and renderer working for homeowners, landlords and commercial clients — internal plastering done smooth and flat, and external rendering that protects and lifts a property.

S/01 · Internal

Plastering

Clean, flat plaster ready to decorate — from a single ceiling to a full house or commercial fit-out.

  • Skimming & re-skimming over old or new
  • Plasterboarding & dry lining
  • Ceilings, walls & patch repairs
  • Polished plaster & media-wall features
S/02 · External

Rendering

A hard-wearing, weatherproof finish that transforms the outside of a property and keeps it protected.

  • Silicone / through-coloured render
  • Sand & cement, scratch & float
  • Pebbledash & textured finishes
  • Render repairs & re-renders
