About

Stone, learned the old way.

Independent stone mason based in Preston. Twenty years on the trowel, the last ten running a one-man operation across Lancashire and the Ribble Valley.

Most of the work is repeat business and word-of-mouth. Garden walls for one-off cottages, full elevations for new builds and extensions, dry stone restoration on the moors. Whatever the job, it’s about reading the stone, lifting the right piece, and locking it in for good.

No subcontractors, no quick fixes. If lime mortar is right for your property, we’ll specify lime mortar. If a wall needs to come down and go back up properly, we’ll quote it that way.
