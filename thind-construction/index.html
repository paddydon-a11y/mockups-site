What we do

One builder for the whole job

Thind Construction covers every stage of a build, from structure to services to finish, for homeowners and commercial clients across Bedford and Bedfordshire. One managed team, so you're not chasing five different trades.

Extensions & Structural Work

Single-storey extensions, knock-throughs, steel beams and load-bearing walls, stud partitions and conversions that add real space.

Kitchens & Bathrooms

Full kitchen and bathroom fit-outs: units, worktops, tiling, plumbing and electrics, all finished and ready to use.

Plastering & Rendering

Skimming, re-plastering, dry-lining and external render: the part that makes the whole job look right.

Flooring, Tiling & UFH

Floor and wall tiling, LVT and laminate, plus wet underfloor heating laid level and built to last.

Groundworks & Drainage

Foundations, footings, excavation, soil pipes and below-ground drainage done right before anything goes up.

Driveways, Patios & Landscaping

Porcelain and natural-stone patios, paths, driveways, fencing and garden rooms that finish the outside off.
