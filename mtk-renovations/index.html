About MTK

Builders who see the whole job through

MTK Renovations is a Garston-based building outfit covering all aspects of house building across South Liverpool. Because we keep bricklaying, roofing, plastering, kitchens, windows and tiling in-house, your project stays with one team start to finish, no chasing a chain of subbies.

From a single re-roof to a full new build, we work tidy, keep you in the loop and hand back a job we are happy to put our name to.
