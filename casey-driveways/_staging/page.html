A family-run business operated by brothers Stephen and Patrick Casey, and good friend Ben Butler, we bring a decade of experience creating beautiful driveways, patios, brickwork, garden fencing & more.

Casey Driveways & Landscaping operates across Solihull, Birmingham, Coventry, Redditch, and the wider West Midlands area.

We pride ourselves on our friendly and reliable service, keen pricing, high quality of craftmanship, and the speed at which we’ll lovingly bring your vision to reality.
