Chapter 04 — How We Work

Four steps. No surprises.

From the first conversation to the day you hand over the keys, the process is the same — transparent, sequenced, written down.

01.

Consult & brief.

We meet on site or in the studio. We listen to the brief, measure up, and ask the awkward questions now rather than later.

02.

Design & cost.

Concept drawings, material choices and a fixed-price proposal. You see exactly what you're getting before anyone swings a hammer.

03.

Build & project-manage.

One site manager, one programme, weekly updates. We coordinate every trade, so you don't have to.

04.

Handover & aftercare.

Snag walk, certificates, handover pack. We stay on the phone for 12 months — nothing gets left dangling.
