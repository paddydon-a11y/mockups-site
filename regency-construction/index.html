What We Do

Our Services

01

Extensions

Our groundworkers, bricklayers, carpenters and plasterers will build the extension you’ve always dreamed of — designed to blend seamlessly with your existing property.

02

Renovations

Complete interior and exterior renovations to breathe new life into your property. From structural alterations to high-end finishes — we handle it all in-house.

03

Loft Conversions

Our team of carpenters, roofers, plumbers and electricians carry out loft conversions from design stage through to full completion, adding valuable living space to your home.

04

New Builds

We offer a complete design and build service for new dwellings, or we’re equally happy to work with your existing plans. From plot to handover — managed every step of the way.

05

Garage Conversions

Transform underused garage space into a functional living area, home office, or guest suite. Full planning, building regs, and premium finish as standard.

06

Design & Planning

Our in-house design team handles everything from initial concept to full planning approval. We oversee every detail so your vision becomes reality.

How We Work

From Vision to Reality

1

Consultation

We meet to discuss your project, understand your vision, and assess what’s possible within your budget and timeline.

2

Design & Planning

Our design team creates detailed plans and handles all planning applications, building regs, and structural calculations.

3

Build Phase

Our skilled tradesmen bring the design to life with meticulous attention to detail, keeping you informed every step of the way.

4

Handover

Final inspections, snagging, certificates issued, and your 10-year insurance backed guarantee — then we hand you the keys.
