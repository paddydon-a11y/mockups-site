How a job runs

The four stages of a flawless finish.

Survey & quote

We visit, measure up, talk through materials and give you a clear, no-obligation price.

Strip & prepare

Old tiles out, surfaces levelled, decoupling and tanking where it's needed. The bit that matters most.

Set & cut

Tiles laid to your chosen pattern, every cut measured around sockets, pipes and corners.

Grout & seal

Grouted, sealed, silicone lines run clean — then a full clean-down before we leave.
