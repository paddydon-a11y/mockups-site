Capabilities

One team, one number.

Plumbing, tiling, plastering and finish: all carried out by the same core team, so there is no hand-off between trades and no finger-pointing at handover. Below, a full list.

  • 01.Full Bathroom RenovationsEnd-to-end refits, including strip-out, first fix plumbing, tiling, second fix, rendering and decorative finish.
  • 02.Tiling & StoneworkLarge-format porcelain, marble effect, herringbone, subway, mosaic, Moroccan pattern, natural stone. Wet walls and floors.
  • 03.Showers, Baths & ScreensLow-profile trays, standard and walk-in showers, crittall-style screens, standard and freestanding baths. Thermostatic mixers fitted correctly.
  • 04.Taps, Toilets & BasinsWall-hung, back-to-wall, pedestal. Chrome, brushed, matte black. Supplied or client specified, all fitted level and leak-tested.
  • 05.Radiators & Towel RailsHeated towel rails, vertical feature radiators, traditional column and compact panel. Plumbed in copper or plastic per spec.
  • 06.Pipework & Soil StacksFirst fix pipework in copper or plastic, internal soil stacks replaced or rerouted, all to current building regulations.
  • 07.Plastering & RenderingThrough our sister trade, Sycamore Plastering. Skim, full re-plaster and external render, ready for paint first time.
  • 08.Guttering & RainwareExternal guttering, downpipes and rainware installed or replaced. Soffits and fascias on request.

The Process

From first site visit to key back.

I.

Site Visit

Free, no-obligation walk of the space. We measure, note the constraints, and listen to what you actually need.

II.

Specification

Written quote with sanitary ware options, tile routes, pipework notes and a firm programme. Transparent and itemised.

III.

The Build

Strip-out, first fix, plaster, tile, second fix and finish by the same team. Dust sheets, protective floor covering, tidy at each day's end.

IV.

Handover

Leak tested, snag walked, certificates where applicable. A genuine aftercare ear for 12 months beyond sign-off.
