What we do

Brickwork,
start to finish.

Four things on the menu. I keep it tight on purpose so you get a tradesman who knows the job inside out, not a one-man band winging it. Same standard whether it's a single wall or a full new build.

01

New Build Brickwork

Plot brickwork on new build and development sites. Block inner skin, cavity insulation, clean facing brick, courses pinged off a string line and gauged the whole way up.

Facing brickBlockworkCavity workDevelopments
02

Brick & Block Extensions

Single-storey extensions, side returns, garage builds and outbuildings. Working off architect's drawings or building reg specs, brick face matched to the existing house.

Single-storeySide returnGarageOutbuildings
03

Garden & Retaining Walls

Boundary walls, retaining walls, planter walls and capped feature walls. Foundations dug to depth, DPC laid, weep holes set, piers and copings finished sharp.

BoundaryRetainingCappedSoldier course
04

Repairs & Repointing

Cracked walls, settled lintels, frost-damaged courses, blown render. Raked out by hand and repointed in lime or sand-cement to match the original. Weatherstruck, bucket-handle or flush, your call.

About Tarwinder

Borderline
obsessive about
a straight line.

I started TS Brickwork because I was tired of seeing other lads' jobs done in a hurry. I take on what I can finish well, I'm there from the first dig to the last brush, and the line on the wall stays straight whether anyone's watching or not.

I work with the same suppliers every job, source bricks to match what's already there, and clean up after myself. If you don't like the look of a course, we knock it out and lay it again. That's the deal.
