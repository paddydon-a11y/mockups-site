(Combo H-split-stacked + S-left-accent-border + G-asymmetric-mosaic — not in banned combos list.)

- **Surface:** WARM PLASTER light — warm cream `#f4efe6` / `#faf7f1`, deep-navy ink. Reads like fresh
  set plaster; warm, clean, trustworthy local tradesman.
- **Accent:** royal blue `#2050c8` / deep `#173da0` (off the van's blue panel) — primary, trustworthy.
  Secondary warm **terracotta** `#cf6b34` (off the van's orange house mark + fresh-plaster pink) used
  sparingly for keylines / the "set plaster" tone. Distinct from banned palette families.
- **Display type:** Bricolage Grotesque (sturdy, characterful grotesk; NOT in banned window —
  Space Grotesk / Schibsted / Archivo / Familjen / Hanken / Outfit etc. all banned/saturated).
- **Body type:** Inter.
- **Flourish (NEW loader concept — "plaster cure"):** a wall panel starts as wet, darker plaster
  (warm brown). A soft diagonal cure-front + a travelling sheen highlight pass across it and the panel
  resolves to smooth, *set* pale-pink plaster, then the "NC" wordmark + "Plastering & General Building ·
  Bristol" subline settle. A MATERIAL state-change loader (wet→set), deliberately NOT a trowel/roller/
  pointing/chisel tool loader (all of those are banned).
