What we do

Renovation, all aspects

Whether it's one tired room or a house from top to bottom, NP Home Renovation handles the lot, all managed by one team so you're not chasing five different trades.

Full House Renovations

Whole-property refurbishments, top to bottom: structural work, walls, floors, the lot.

Kitchens

Full kitchen fit-outs: units, worktops, tiling, plumbing and electrics, finished and ready to use.

Bathrooms

Bathrooms and en-suites: full rip-out and refit, wet rooms, tiling and waterproofing.

Extensions & Conversions

Single-storey extensions, garage conversions and loft rooms that add real space.

Plastering & Decorating

Skimming, re-plastering, painting and finishing: the part that makes it all look right.

Flooring & Tiling

Floor and wall tiling, LVT, laminate and engineered wood, laid level and lasting.
