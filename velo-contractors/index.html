01 / What we do

Reinforcement, end to end

From scheduling and cut & bend supply through to fixed cages and finished pours — a single reinforcement package for main contractors and developers.

S/01

Steel Fixing

Placing and tying reinforcement to drawings — slabs, columns, walls, cores and beams, fixed accurately and ready for inspection.

S/02

Rebar Supply & Scheduling

Reinforced Concrete Frames

Substructures & Groundworks

Couplers & Splicing

Mechanical splicing and rebar couplers for continuity at construction joints, kickers and heavily congested connections.

Labour Supply

03 / How we work

From schedule to pour

01 · Take-off

Drawings & schedule

We work from your engineer's drawings, prepare bar bending schedules and agree quantities and sequence.

02 · Supply

Cut & bend

Reinforcement is supplied to BS 8666 and delivered phased to match the pour programme — no clutter on site.

03 · Fix

Place & tie

Carded gangs place and tie the steel to drawings, with covers, laps and spacers right first time.

04 · Sign-off

Inspect & pour

We present each pour for inspection and hand over a clean, compliant cage ready for concrete.

Velo Contractors Ltd is a specialist steel-fixing and rebar contractor working with main contractors and developers on major construction projects across the UK.

We handle reinforcement as a complete package — scheduling and cut & bend supply, fixed cages to cores, columns, slabs and substructures, couplers and mechanical splicing, and supply of carded steel-fixing gangs where you need extra labour on the programme.

Every cage is fixed to drawings and to BS 4449 / BS 8666, presented clean for inspection and ready for the pour — the kind of work you can see across our project gallery.
