What Alan Does

Welding and repairs, wherever the job is.

From getting a car through its MOT to a new set of driveway gates, Alan brings the kit to you across North Wales and Chester.

MOT Welding Repairs

Sills, floor pans, subframes and chassis welded up solid to get your car or van through its MOT. Quick turnaround for retests.

Car, Van & Transit Repair

Bodywork and structural welding for cars, vans and Transits. Rust, holes, exhausts and brackets sorted at your home or yard.

Gates & Driveway Gates

Driveway and garden gates made to measure, repaired or re-hung. Built in steel to last and finished to suit the property.

Railings & Handrails

Garden, step and balcony railings fabricated and fitted. Galvanised or painted, square and safe, made to fit the space.

On-Site Mobile Welding

Full mobile rig and generator. Alan welds where the work is, so there is no towing a vehicle in or hauling steel about.

Fabrication & Maintenance

Brackets, frames, trailer repairs and one-off metalwork. Bring a drawing or a back-of-an-envelope sketch and Alan will build it.

About Alan

A local welder you can actually get hold of.

Alan Oates Welding & Maintenance is a mobile outfit based in Mold, Flintshire, covering North Wales and across to Chester. Alan brings the welder and the rig straight to your door, whether that is a driveway, a yard or the side of the road.

From welding a car back through its MOT to building a set of gates from scratch, it is one fella who turns up, does the job properly and charges a fair price. No call centres, no being passed around.

  • Fully mobile, welding at your home, yard or roadside.
  • Vehicles, gates, railings and general maintenance, big or small.
  • Straight quotes and quick turnarounds, especially for MOT retests.
