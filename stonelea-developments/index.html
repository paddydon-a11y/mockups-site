About Stonelea

Quality craftsmanship, built on trust

Stonelea Developments is a trusted general builder based in Rotherham, delivering quality construction across South Yorkshire. We specialise in barn conversions, stone restorations, new builds, extensions and structural work.

From heritage stone properties requiring careful restoration to modern new builds with the latest specifications — we bring the same level of care, skill and attention to detail to every project.

We manage the full build process from foundations and groundwork through to the finished product, ensuring every stage meets our exacting standards.

“We build it as if it were our own.”

What we do

From heritage restorations to contemporary new builds, every project is completed to the highest standard.

  • 01

    Barn Conversions

    Transforming traditional barns and agricultural buildings into stunning modern living spaces. Sensitive restoration that preserves character whilst meeting contemporary building standards.

  • 02

    Stone Restorations

    Expert restoration of Yorkshire stone properties. Repointing, stone replacement, lime mortar work and structural repairs to heritage and period buildings throughout South Yorkshire.

  • 03

    New Builds

    Complete new build construction from foundations to handover. Residential homes and developments built with quality materials and meticulous attention to detail.

  • 04

    Extensions & Renovations

    Single and double storey extensions, loft conversions, and full property renovations. Seamless integration with your existing home, adding space and value.

  • 05

    Structural Work

    Steel framing, timber roof structures, blockwork, reinforced concrete foundations and all associated groundwork. The backbone of every quality build.

  • 06

    Groundwork & Foundations

    Reinforced foundations, concrete slabs, drainage and all site preparation. Proper groundwork is the foundation of a lasting build — we never cut corners.
