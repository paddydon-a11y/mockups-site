Capability · 01

What we deliver, plot by plot.

01 / Feature

Design & quantification from issued drawings

Plumbing and heating layouts taken from architect's package, costed and scheduled per plot. Material take-offs handed to the builder or nominated merchant ready to call off.

02

Plot-specific kit scheduling

Every plot gets its own pack: 1st-fix, 2nd-fix, commissioning. Fit-out flows with the build sequence, no plot starts without a confirmed kit.

03

Site supervision

Dedicated non-working foreman: programme, quality, plot sign-off. One number for the SM to ring.

04

Pre-plaster & pre-handover audits

Two recorded inspections per plot. Photo log, snag tracker, signed off before plasterers and before keys.

05

Defect response <48h

Service-level cover from sale to end of NHBC. Calls logged, attended, closed out without chasing.

06

Programme coordination

Sequenced against brick, plaster, decoration. Weekly look-ahead, monthly progress to commercial.

07

Tools & site equipment

Small tools, ladders, test gear and PPE all carried in. Builder supplies materials only.

08

Quality control procedures

Documented at intake. Audit trail per plot, photo evidence, sign-off sheets straight to the SM.
