Capability · 01

One studio, every trade.

01 / Feature

Design & visualisation from a home survey

We measure on site, listen to how the room is actually lived in, and come back with a design package: plan, elevations, photoreal renders and a fully costed specification before a single tool comes out.

02

Bespoke cabinetry & joinery

Hand-built in our workshop. Painted shaker, hardwood, slab fronts in veneer or stained oak. Drawer pull-outs, integrated appliances and inset stone all detailed in.

03

Plumbing & heating

Gas Safe and qualified across heating, underfloor, drainage and pressurised hot water. We build the room around the plumbing, never the other way round.

04

Stone, tiling & finishes

Marble, quartzite and porcelain templated, cut and installed by our own fitters. Bookmatched runs, mitred edges, drainage falls properly set in wet rooms.

05

Lighting & electrical

NICEIC-registered. Layered scene lighting, integrated under-cabinet, IP-rated bathroom circuits and smart-home tie-ins designed at the rendering stage.

06

Wet rooms & walk-ins

Frame-out, tank, screed to fall, drain set, bookmatched stone and frameless glass. Built dry once and never revisited.

07

Project management

Single point of contact from survey to handover. Weekly photo updates, snag tracker, trades scheduled so you're never living through silence on site.

08

Aftercare & warranty

Two-year workmanship warranty, manufacturer warranties registered for you, and a dedicated aftercare line for anything that needs settling-in attention.

Process · 03

From first visit to final reveal.

01
Stage 01

Home consultation

We visit, measure and listen. Brief, budget bracket and timing agreed before any design work begins.

02
Stage 02

Design & renders

Plan, elevations and photoreal visuals. Two rounds of revisions included as standard.

03
Stage 03

Specification & quote

Itemised specification with stone, tile, fixture and appliance choices. Fixed-price proposal, no scope creep.

04
Stage 04

Build & install

Single team on site. Strip-out, first fix, joinery, stone, second fix and finish, weekly photo updates.

05
Stage 05

Snag & aftercare

Walk-through with you, snag list closed, two-year warranty registered, aftercare line live.
