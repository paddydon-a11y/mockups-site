What We Do

From roof repairs to full re-roofs, fascias, soffits and guttering, we cover all aspects of domestic roofing across Wrexham and North Wales.

Roof Repairs & Re-Roofing

Complete re-roofs and reliable repairs for all roof types. New felt, battening and tiles fitted to the highest standard.

Fascias, Soffits & Guttering

Full replacement of fascia boards, soffits and guttering. uPVC systems fitted cleanly and built to last.

Roof Lining & Felt

Professional roof lining and felt installation. Breathable membranes and quality materials for a dry, well-ventilated roof.

Ridge Tiles & Lead Work

Ridge tile re-bedding, re-pointing and lead flashing work. Keeping your roof weathertight and looking sharp.

Flat Roofing & Fibreglass

GRP fibreglass and EPDM flat roofing for extensions, garages and dormers. Far superior to old-style felt, built to last decades.
