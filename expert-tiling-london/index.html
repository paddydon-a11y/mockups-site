Tiling is the last trade in. It has to be right.

Expert Tiling London is run by Pawel, a tiler who measures twice and sets out every wall and floor before a single tile is fixed. Plumbers and builders across North London bring us in to finish their jobs, because the tiling is what the customer sees every day.
