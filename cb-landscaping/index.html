What we do.

Patios

Sandstone, porcelain, riven slabs. Dug out, sub-based, jointed properly.

Drainage

French drains, soakaways, channels, manhole repairs. Done before the slabs go down.

Fencing

Closeboard, featheredge, gates and posts concreted in. Made to last in heavy soil.

Lawns & turf

Lifted, levelled, screened topsoil, fresh turf. Edged with kerb or steel.

Pergolas

Steel posts, hardwood beams, on a proper concrete base. Built to take real weather.

Brickwork

Garden walls, retainers, piers, planters. Bonded clean, pointed flush.

Garden maintenance

Hedge cuts, gutter clears, seasonal tidies. Same lads each visit, no subcontracting.

Groundworks

Footings, slabs, services trenches, mini-digger work. The bit that holds the rest up.
