Patios

About

Run by Nathan. Same hands on every job.

Eight years building gardens, patios and drainage runs around Cambridge. CB started on driveways and groundworks for builders, and grew sideways into the soft-landscaping side as the same families kept ringing back.

Nothing on this site was subcontracted out. If you book CB, the lads who turn up are the same lads who give you the quote, and Nathan is on every site at some point in the week.
