What Neil does
/ 01

Nine trades a small build needs, all in one number.

Plastering pays the bills. But most weeks the van also carries fence panels, flat-pack instructions, a couple of shelves and whatever else the customer can't get one trade to take on.

01
Internal plastering
Skim, fresh plaster, patches and coving. Flat finish, ready for paint inside 48 hours.
02
External rendering
Sand-cement and silicone render, repairs, full re-renders. Sealed, breathable, no cracks.
03
Fencing & gate repairs
Panels swapped, posts re-set in concrete, new timber gates hung true.
04
Boiler cupboards
Built from scratch around the boiler. MDF carcass, lipped doors, painted in.
05
Boxing in pipes & meters
Radiator pipes, soil stacks, meter cabinets. Boxed neat, ready for paint.
06
Shelving & alcove fits
Alcove shelves, fitted boards, brackets, garage racking. Solid, level, painted.
07
Loft hatch & ladder fitting
Hatches re-cut, insulated, trimmed. Ladders fitted level on first attempt.
08
Flat-pack fitting
Wardrobes, sideboards, beds, office desks. Built the way the instructions meant.
09
Property maintenance
One call for the list of small jobs most trades will not pick up.

About Neil

Twenty years on the trowel. Still picks up the phone.

Neil started plastering on the North Wales coast two decades ago and never quite stopped. Most days are skim and render. The other days are whatever the householder can't get a single trade to come out for. A fence panel, a flat-pack, a cupboard that needs to go over the boiler before the new tenants move in.
