Who we are

Highland land management, done the safe way

CJ Flail Services is a Highland-based outfit running a remote-controlled tracked flail mower. It works ground that is too steep or too dangerous for a tractor or a man with a strimmer, while the operator stays safely off the slope in full control.

We cut with care for the ground, the watercourses and the wildlife around it, leaving land tidy, workable and ready for whatever comes next.
