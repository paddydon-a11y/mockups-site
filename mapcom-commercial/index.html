About Mapcom

Groundworks to garden makeovers. One team, one phone number.

Mapcom Commercial Ltd is run by Matthew Pitchforth, a groundworker who got fed up with jobs being split across four different trades who never turned up on the same day. So we built a team that handles everything from the dig to the final brush of resin.

Driveways, drainage, concrete pads, garden patios, infiltration tanks, sub-base, edging, finish. Residential, commercial, industrial yards, new-build plots. If it needs digging, levelling or laying, it is what we do.

What We Do

Everything from the sub-base up.

Eight core services under one roof. Every job priced clearly, every job signed off properly.

01

Resin Driveways

Permeable resin-bound driveways in any colour. Poured over a properly prepped sub-base so it lasts.

02

Tarmac

Fresh tarmac drives, car parks and access roads. Hot-rolled, rolled in, finished with a proper edge.

03

Block Paved Driveways

Traditional and contemporary block paving. Dropped kerbs, soldier course edges, kiln-dried sand finish.

04

Garden Renovations

Full garden makeovers, patios, retaining walls, lawns and planting. Designed and built as one project.

05

Concrete Pad Replacement

Old cracked pad ripped out, new slab poured, reinforced and finished. Perfect for sheds, garages and workshops.

06

Groundworks in Full

New-build foundations, dig-outs, service trenches, levelling and sub-base. Commercial and residential sites.

07

Drainage

Foul and surface water drainage, gullies, manholes, soakaways and connections. CCTV checks and remedials.

08

Infiltration Tanks

SUDS-compliant infiltration and attenuation tanks for new builds and hard-standing surface water management.

How We Work

Four steps, no surprises.

Same process every time, whether it is a single drive or a full commercial dig-out.

01

Site Visit

Matthew comes out within a few days, walks the job with you, takes levels and photos. No pressure, no hard sell.

02

Fixed Quote

Itemised quote within 48 hours. Fixed price on the dig, the materials, the finish. No surprises mid-job.

03

On Site

Plant delivered, site fenced, clean access kept to the house. Daily sweep-down, weekly catch-ups.

04

Snag & Sign-Off

We snag our own work before handover. Guarantee in writing. Drainage certified and signed off where required.
