What We Do

Six things we do every single week.

Anything in wood, in and around the home. Hand-built where it matters, fitted neatly, finished properly.

Bespoke Kitchen Fitting

Full kitchen installs and refits. Cabinets, worktops, splashbacks, plumbing-in and second-fix carpentry, all under one roof.

Hardwood & Softwood Floors

Solid oak, engineered, parquet and pine. Supplied, fitted, sanded and sealed. Repairs and restoration too.

Fitted Wardrobes & Storage

Built-in wardrobes, alcove units, under-stair storage, dressing rooms. Made to measure, fitted on site.

Windows & Doors

Sash windows, casements, internal and external doors. Repairs, draught-proofing, full replacements where needed.

Architraves & Skirting

Period mouldings matched to original profiles, modern skirting and architrave runs, full house installs and repairs.

Small Renovations

Bathroom and kitchen rejigs, partition walls, floor levelling, second-fix on extensions. The carpentry side of small builds.

About Alex

One carpenter, four decades, a small black book of regulars.

I'm Alex Constantina. I've been a carpenter and joiner in North London for over forty years. I started out doing repairs and second-fix work and over the years built up enough skill to take on whole kitchens, fitted bedrooms, hardwood floors, period sash windows, internal staircases, pretty much anything in wood you'd want done in your home.

I work with a small, trusted team and I'm on every job myself. No middlemen, no flashy sales pitch. You get an honest quote, a clear timeline, and work that gets finished properly.
