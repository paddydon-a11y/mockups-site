A local fitter who treats your home like our own.

K.E Bathrooms is an independent bathroom installer based in Liphook. We handle the whole job from first sketch to final seal — plumbing, tiling, panelling, electrics and finishing — so you only ever deal with one trusted team.

Whether it's a sleek marble-effect shower room, a practical family bathroom or a low-threshold wet room for easier access, every project is fitted properly, kept tidy, and finished to a standard we're proud to put our name to.
