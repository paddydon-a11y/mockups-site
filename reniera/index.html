From WhatsApp to handover, five stages.

Most jobs start within 14 days.

Site visit & chat

Klodjan visits, measures, and listens. No salesman. We sketch ideas there and then so you know what you're getting before you commit.

Written quote

Itemised by trade, materials specified by brand, dates committed in writing. No "extras" mid-build for things you assumed were included.

Floors covered, walls sheeted, skip and welfare arranged. Neighbours notified. The site is the team's responsibility, not yours.
