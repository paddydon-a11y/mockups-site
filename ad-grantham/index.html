Meet Alex Grantham

AD Grantham is run hands-on by Alex Grantham, based in Kent and working right across the South East. It grew out of being the person people called when they needed something sorted: a clearance, a repair, a load shifting, sometimes all three in the same week.

When you get in touch, you deal with Alex from start to finish. The work is done with care, the site is left clean and tidy, and everything is covered by Public Liability Insurance, so if the unforeseen happens you are protected.
