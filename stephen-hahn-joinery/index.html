About

One-man outfit. One standard.

Ste Hahn runs Stephen Hahn Joinery on his own out of Whitworth, OL12. He draws the job up, makes the joinery and fits it himself. No subcontractors handed half a kitchen and a vague drawing.

Same person quoting, same person at handover. He turns up when he says, works clean, and stays till it's right. That's the whole pitch.
