About BBS

Barry-based. Family-run. Trusted across South Wales.

Boudot Building Services is run by Eric Boudot out of 6 Ewenny Close, Barry. What started as one man on the tools has become a small, trusted firm specialising in the two rooms that matter most, your kitchen and your bathroom, plus loft and garage conversions that add real value to your home.

Every job handled start to finish. Design, materials, plumbing, tiling, electrics, plastering, finishing. One point of contact, one team, one price. The finish speaks for itself, and our customers say it louder than we would.

How We Work

Four steps. No surprises.

The same honest process every time, whether it is a new suite or a full loft.

01

Visit

Eric comes out to the house within a few days. Walks the job, answers every question. No pressure, no hard sell.

02

Quote

Written quote within the week. Fixed price where we can, broken down line by line so you see exactly where the money goes.
