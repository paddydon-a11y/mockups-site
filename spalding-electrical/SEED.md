- **Surface:** INDUSTRIAL DARK — graphite near-black `#0c1014` / `#10161c`, panels `#151c24`. Reads
  switchgear / control-panel / heavy-current. Deliberately dark (most electrical mockups here are light or
  blue-on-white; the few dark ones use different accents).
- **Accent:** live-cyan `#1ec8e6` → `#16b6d6` (current/voltage glow) with a copper `#c47b46` micro-accent
  (bus-bar / wiring). NOT ocean-blue / cobalt / ice-blue (banned or saturated electrical blues) and not
  the dark+yellow used by Deluxe/Barkerend/MTN. Cyan-on-graphite = "energised / live".
- **Display type:** Chakra Petch — squared technical grotesk, circuit-board feel (NOT in the banned last-10
  window: Schibsted/Marcellus/Hanken/Space Grotesk/Cinzel/Anton/Big Shoulders/Plus Jakarta/Fraunces/Clash).
- **Body type:** Inter.
- **Flourish (NEW loader concept):** "current trace power-on" — a cyan current pulse runs left-to-right
  along an angular PCB/circuit trace, reaches a contact node that snaps closed, the node + an indicator
  lamp light up cyan (powers on), then the "SPALDING ELECTRICAL · Lincolnshire" wordmark settles. Electrical
  energise loader; distinct from all banned loaders (no trowel/roller/pointing/chisel/fence/spectrum-bars).
