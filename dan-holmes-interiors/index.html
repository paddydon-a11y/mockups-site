Our craft

Furniture made by hand, to fit one home, yours.

At Dan Holmes Interiors we design, build and fit every piece ourselves, from a full bespoke kitchen to a single run of fitted wardrobes. Nothing is flat-packed and nothing is off the shelf.

It starts with a conversation about how you actually use the space, then becomes a drawing, then real cabinetry cut and assembled in our workshop, and finally fitted to the millimetre in your home.

What we make

One workshop, every room.

If it's fitted, painted and built to last, we make it. Each piece is designed for its room rather than forced to fit.

01

Bespoke Kitchens

Handmade in-frame and shaker kitchens, designed around your space, your cooking and your storage, then painted in any colour you like.

02

Fitted Bedrooms

Wardrobes and dressing rooms built wall to wall and floor to ceiling, with interiors fitted out exactly how you want them.

03

Home Offices

Fitted desks, shelving and storage that turn a spare room into a study you actually want to work in.

04

Storage Solutions

Understairs cupboards, boot rooms, alcoves and media units, the clever joinery that finally makes a room work.

05

Utility & Boot Rooms

Hard-working laundry and boot rooms with the same handmade cabinetry as the kitchen they sit beside.

06

Living & Media Walls

Fitted alcoves, bookcases and media walls that frame a fireplace or television and use every inch of a room.

How it works

From first visit to final fit.

01

Design Visit

We come to you, measure up and talk through how you live in the space and what you want from it.

02

Design & Quote

You get a considered design, real drawings and a clear, itemised quote with no surprises.

03

Handmade

Every cabinet is cut, built and painted by hand in our workshop, not ordered in flat-packed.

04

Fitted

We install it ourselves, scribed and finished to the millimetre, and leave the place clean.
