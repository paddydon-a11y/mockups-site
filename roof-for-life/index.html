What We Do

Flat and pitched roofs are our bread and butter, but Freddy and the team are set up to handle the whole roofline. Felt, fibreglass, sika or lead, we'll spec the right system for the job.

Flat Roofing

Felt, fibreglass (GRP), Sika liquid systems and traditional lead. Every new flat roof comes with a written guarantee.

Pitched Roofing

Full re-roofs, new build pitched roofs, strip-and-retiles, slate work. New battens and breathable membranes fitted properly.

Roof Repairs

Slipped tiles, leaks, storm damage and one-offs. Honest about what's a patch and what needs more.

Lead & Chimneys

Lead flashing, valleys, soakers and chimney rebuilds. The bits that fail first done with the right metal and the right detail.

Family Run. One Promise.

Roof For Life is Freddy Fuller and a small team out of Canterbury. We've been on roofs across East Kent since 2018, doing the work the way our grandads were taught and the way we'd want it done on our own house.

Every new roof we put down comes with a written guarantee. We don't sub-contract, we don't disappear, and we don't quote what we can't back up. If we can't do something properly, we'll tell you, and we'll often know someone who can.
