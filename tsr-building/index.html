What we do

Eight trades under one tidy roof.

Most South East London homes need more than one trade. Roofers who only do roofs leave you chasing painters, cleaners and a skip. We turn up with the lot — so a leaky gutter and a tired-looking front of house become one phone call.

01 / FLAGSHIP

Roof Repairs

Slipped tiles, lead flashing, ridge work, chimney pointing and storm damage. We climb, photograph the issue and quote properly — no scaring you into a full re-roof you don't need.

02

Gutter Repairs

Leaks, sags, splits, missing brackets and broken downpipes — fixed neatly.

03

Gutter Cleaning

Vacuum-cleared from the ground. Camera footage on request.

04 / EXTERIOR

Exterior Painting

Render, masonry, sash windows, soffits, fascias, front doors. We mask, prep, and repaint properly so it lasts more than one wet winter.

05

Exterior Cleaning

Render, brickwork, paths and patios soft-washed back to bright.

06

Carpet Cleaning

Hot-water extraction. Rooms dry within hours, not days.

07

Interior Deep Cleans

End-of-tenancy, post-builders' dust, spring resets — top to bottom.

08

Waste Clearance

Garden, garage, loft and full-house clears. Licensed and ticketed.
