What we do

Groundworks,
start to finish.

From the first cut of the ground to the finished slab, Luke Stacey covers every stage of the groundworks, so you deal with one team from start to finish.

Footings

Dug, formed and poured to spec for extensions, new builds and garden rooms.

Foundations

Strip, trench-fill and raft foundations laid level and to building control spec.

Excavation

Site dig-outs, reduced levels and muck away with our own diggers and dumpers.

Concrete & Slabs

Oversite slabs, garage bases and hardstanding poured, tamped and finished.

Drainage

New runs, soakaways, repairs and connections, surveyed and tested.

Demolition & Clearance

Safe, tidy demolition and full site clearance, muck away included.

Driveways & Hardstanding

Sub-bases, tarmac, block paving and resin, edged and finished to last.

Reinstatement

Ground, verges and surfaces made good and left exactly as found.

About Luke Stacey

Cambridgeshire groundworkers
who turn up and graft.

Luke Stacey Groundworks is a Cambridgeshire-based groundworks outfit working for homeowners, builders and developers across the county. With our own diggers, dumpers and operators, we take on the full job, footings, foundations, excavation, drainage, concrete, demolition and driveways.
