03 — About

Halifax scaffolders.
Proper Yorkshire graft.

AJ Scaffolding is a Halifax-based, family-run scaffolding company with over 10 years in the trade. We came up on West Yorkshire stone — terraces, mills, chapels — and we know what it takes to get a safe, solid structure up around the buildings this part of the world is made of.

We work for homeowners, main contractors, roofers, builders and estate agents. Every job is priced fair, signed off, and built to stand up to whatever the Pennines can throw at it. Nothing flashy — just proper work, done properly.
