How it works

Calm, considered, unhurried.

I only take on one job at a time. No overlap, no juggling, no three-week disappearing acts.

01

A proper chat

I visit, measure up, look at what you've got. We talk colours, brands, budget, timings — over a coffee.

02

Clear written quote

Itemised, fixed-price, usually within a week. What's included, what isn't, and when we could start.

03

One pair of hands

I do the work myself — no subbies on a first-name basis you've never met. Daily updates if you're not home.

04

A clean handover

Walk-through together, snag list, care instructions, and a tidy house back. Two-year cover on the workmanship.
