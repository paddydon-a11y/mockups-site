What We Do

Domestic electrical services, big or small.

Whether it's a single socket, a full rewire or a smart lighting and energy upgrade, every job is completed safely, cleanly and to a high standard.

Rewires & Consumer Units

Full and partial rewires, fuse board upgrades and modern consumer units to keep your home safe and up to standard.

Lighting & LED Design
